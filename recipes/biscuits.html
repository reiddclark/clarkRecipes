Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 2 cups flour

Instructions

Sift four, salt, baking powder, cream of tartar, and sugar; cut in shortening until mixture resembles course crumbs; add milk all at once; stir just until dough follows fork around bowl; pat or roll 3/4 " thick; cut with biscuit cutter; bake at 450* for 10-12 min.
