Reid's Famous Coleslaw

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/8 teaspoon Pepper
  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Buttermilk
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon Lemon juice
  • 8 cups Cabbage, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Carrot, shredded
  • 2 tablespoon Onion, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon Vinegar

Instructions

1. Be sure cabbage and carrots are chopped up into very fine pieces (about the size of rice).

2. Combine the mayonaise, sugar, milk, buttermilk, lemon juice, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a large bowl and beat until smooth.

3. Add the cabbage, carrots, and onion, and mix well.

4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
