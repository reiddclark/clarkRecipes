Cider Syrup

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

12 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine, butter or margarine
  • 1 cup apple cider or juice, apple cider or juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon, cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch, cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg, nutmeg

Instructions

Mix sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon and nutmeg in saucepan. Stir in apple cider and lemon juice. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil one minute. Blend in butter or margarine. Serve warm.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 52 Calories; 2g Fat (32.2% calories from fat); trace Protein; 9g Carbohydrate; trace Dietary Fiber; 5mg Cholesterol; 20mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Grain(Starch); 0 Fruit; 1/2 Fat; 1/2 Other Carbohydrates.

NOTES : This is a Thackeray family favorite. I especially like it on oatmeal pancakes.
