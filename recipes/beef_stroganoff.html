Beef Stroganoff

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds round steak
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 pound mushroom-sliced
  • 6 tablespoons flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 6 tablespoons shortening
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • 1 tablespoon brown gravy flavoring
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 nan onion

Instructions

Melt butter, add mushrooms and cook about 3 minutes. Remove. Add onion and cook 3 minutes. Cut steak into strips 1 1/2 inches by 2 1/2 inches. Toss in flour mixed with salt and pepper. (steak is easier to cut if partially frozen)

Put shortening in frying pan. When hot drop in meat. Do not stack meat if you want it to brown quickly without drying out. Put in just what the pan will hold nicely; brown it and take it out, then the rest, etc.

When meat is browned, add or sprinkle with whatever flour is left over. Give meat a stir to mix flour in drippings in pan. Add stock made with 2 bouillon cubes and 2 cups water. Stiff in gravy flavoring, onions and mushrooms. Bake at 350Â° for 1 hour. A few minutes before serving add sour cream. Serve on boiled rice.
