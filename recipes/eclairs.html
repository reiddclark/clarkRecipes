Eclairs

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water, water
  • nan dash salt, salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, butter
  • 1 cup flour, flour
  • 4 nan eggs, eggs

Instructions

Boil water, add butter until melted. Add flour all at once, cook stirring vigorously until mixture forms ball around spoon (only takes a minute). Take off heat, cool 2 to 3 minutes. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating vigorously after each addition. After last egg is added beat until shiny and smooth. Drop by spoonful shaped to long oval. onto greased cookie sheet. Bake 450° for 10 minutes, 325° last 10 minutes. When cool split and add chilled pudding. Frost with chocolate frosting, chill.

Hint- Pudding taste better is some cool whip or whipped cream is added. Use cooked pudding - it tastes better.
