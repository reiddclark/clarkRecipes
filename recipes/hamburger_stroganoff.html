Hamburger Stroganoff

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound noodles, noodles
  • 1 cup sour cream, sour cream
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup, cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 small onion, onion
  • 1 pound hamburger, hamburger

Instructions

Fill large pot with 3 quarts of water, add 2 teaspoon salt. Bring to boil, add the noodles. Cook until noodles are done, drain. While noodles are cooking start the hamburger to brown.Brown hamburger, add onions and cook until they are transparent. Add cream of mushroom soup, cook for 5 minutes. Add sour cream and serve over noodles
