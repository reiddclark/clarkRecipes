Spaghetti Sauce

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

20 Servings

Ingredients

  • 5 8 ounce tomato sauce
  • 5 6 ounce tomato paste
  • 1 lb. country style spare ribs
  • nan nan salt
  • nan nan pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • nan nan garlic salt-twice around pan
  • 1 large can whole tomatoes mashed
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • nan nan pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. oregano
  • 1 tsp. basil
  • 1/2 tsp. parsley flakes
  • 1 tsp. chicken bouillon

Instructions

In Dutch oven: Place 2 tablespoons oil, brown sausage and country ribs.Add salt, pepper, and garlic salt on meat to taste.Take out meat and fry 1 large sliced onion. Add 1 large can whole tomatoes mashed. Bring to boil, then loser heat and add tomato sauce and tomato paste. Then add spices.Start to boil, mix and add meats. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 4 hours. When sauce is cool, put in freezer containers. I usually double recipe and freeze for later.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 94 Calories; 4g Fat (34.2% calories from fat); 4g Protein; 13g Carbohydrate; 3g Dietary Fiber; 9mg Cholesterol; 870mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Grain(Starch); 0 Lean Meat; 2 1/2 Vegetable; 1/2 Fat.
