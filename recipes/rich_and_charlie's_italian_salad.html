Ingredients

  • 1 cup olive oil, olive oil
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, romaine lettuce
  • 1 jar pimento, drained and chopped
  • 1 nan red onion, red onion
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, parmesan cheese
  • 1 can hearts of palm, hearts of palm
  • 1 can artichoke heart, artichoke heart
  • 1 teaspoon dried mustard, dried mustard
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder, garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper, pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt, salt
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar, red wine vinegar
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, iceberg lettuce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar, sugar

Instructions

Mix olive oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, garlic powder and dried mustard together with a whip. Set aside

Wash lettuce and tear into bite-size pieces into a salad bowl. Add remaining ingredients to lettuce. Pour dressing mixture that has been set aside on and toss. Serve immediately.

This makes way too much dressing. You could have it or 1/4 it and still have plenty.
