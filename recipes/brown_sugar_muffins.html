Brown Sugar Muffins

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups berries
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 10 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

Heat oven to 355. In a large bowl mix flour, baking powder, soda and salt. In another bowl cream butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla. Add 1/3 dry ingredients and mix, then 1/3 of buttermilk, until finished. Fold in fruit and fill greased muffin tins. Bake until done, about 15-20 min.
