Frog Eye Salad

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

20 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 teaspoons salt, salt
  • 1 16 ounce pkg acini de pepe, acini de pepe
  • 1 cup coconut, optional
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallow, optional
  • 1 9 oz cool whip, cool whip
  • 1 20oz can pineapple crushed, drained
  • 2 20 oz. pineapple tidbits, drained
  • 3 11 oz. cans mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 tablespoon oil, oil
  • 3 quarts water, water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, lemon juice
  • 1 3/4 cups pineapple juice, pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons flour, flour
  • 1 cup sugar, sugar
  • 2 nan eggs, beaten

Instructions

Combine sugar, flour and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Gradually stir in pineapple juice and eggs. Cook over moderate heat, stirring until thickened. Add lemon juice. Cool mixture to room temperature. Bring water, remaining 2 teaspoons salt and oil to boil. Add acini di pepe. Cook at a rolling boil until acini de pepe is done. Drain acini de pepe, rinse with water, drain again and cool to room temperature. Combine egg mixture and acini de pepe. Mix lightly but thoroughly. Refrigerate overnight in airtight container. Add remaining ingredients. Mix lightly but thoroughly. Refrigerate until chilled in airtight container. Salad may be refrigerated for as long as a week in airtight container.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 101 Calories; 3g Fat (21.8% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 19g Carbohydrate; 1g Dietary Fiber; 19mg Cholesterol; 279mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Grain(Starch); 0 Lean Meat; 1/2 Fruit; 1/2 Fat; 1/2 Other Carbohydrates.
