Gnocchi with Tomato basil

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • .5 cup Basil, chopped
  • 6 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/8 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1 can (16 oz) Tomato, crushed
  • .5 cup Water
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella
  • 1 nan Onion, chopped
  • 1 pd Gnocchi
  • 2 tbs Olive oil

Instructions

Heat oven to 475
Heat oil in ovensafe skillet until simmering. cook gnocchi, stirring, until lightly browned (4 mins) Transfer to plate

Cook onion until soft (3min)stir in garlic and pepper flakes and cook 30 seconds. Stir in Tomatoes and water and cook until slightly thickened about 5 mins

Add bail and Gnocchi to pan. Reduce heat to low and simmer stirring occasionally until gnocchi is tender (5-7 mins). Sprinkle with Mozzarella and bake until cheese is well browned - about 8 mins. ( or do not put in oven and just stir until melted)
