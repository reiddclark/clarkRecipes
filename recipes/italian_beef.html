Italian Beef

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons Oregano, Oregano
  • 8 pounds sirloin tip roast, sirloin tip roast
  • 4 cups water, water
  • 3 cubes beef bouillon, beef bouillon
  • 8 Tablespoons Worcestershire sauce, Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Basil, Basil
  • 3 packages Good Seasons Italian Salad Dressing Mix, Good Seasons Italian Salad Dressing Mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning, Italian seasoning

Instructions

Cook roast at 325* for about 4 hours. Keep covered while cooking. Cool and slice thin. Add to above mixture. Cook at least one hour before serving. Serve juice with the meat. After cooking roast you can take cooled roast back to butcher to slice if you don't have a slicer.
