Corn Chowder

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, roasted
  • 2 tablespoons red onion, chopped
  • 1 pound frozen supper sweet white corn kernels, frozen supper sweet white corn kernels
  • 1/8 pound bacon, fried and crumbled
  • 1 cup fat free half-and-half, fat free half-and-half
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper, red pepper
  • 16 ounces chicken broth, chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter, butter
  • 1/4 cup flour, flour

Instructions

Roast the garlic by wrapping it in foil and place in a hot oven for about 30 minutes; when it is done you can just squeeze it out of the papery skin.Cook the onion in the bacon grease. Drain the remaining grease and add butter, flour, salt, peppers and crushed garlic. Stir quickly to avoid lumps and add the corn and chicken broth. Cover and cook for 5 - 10 minutes. Add the half and half and garnish with tomato chumks.May need to thin with up to 1 cup of milk.I always double this recipe. The orginal recipe calls for 5 ears of corn, kernels sliced off.
