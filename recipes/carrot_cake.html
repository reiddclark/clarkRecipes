Carrot Cake

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

12 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups oil
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, optional
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons soda
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup coconut
  • 8 ounces crushed pineapple
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 4 nan eggs
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups sugar

Instructions

Beat oil, eggs and sugar. Add rest of ingredients. Bake in large tube pan for about one hour. Or in 9 x 13 pan.
