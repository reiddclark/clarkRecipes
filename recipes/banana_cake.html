Banana Cake

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mashed banana
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sour milk
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 2 cups flour

Instructions

1. Cream sugar and butter.
2. Add eggs, sour milk and bananas.
3. Add dry ingredients. Mix.
4. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes or until done.

Can make a 9 x 13 sheet cake or bake it in a cookie sheet for flatter bar type cake.
