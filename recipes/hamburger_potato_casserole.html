Hamburger Potato Casserole

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup evaporated milk, evaporated milk
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese, grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 nan onion, minced
  • 1 pound hamburger, hamburger
  • nan nan salt, salt
  • 5 nan potato, potato
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup, cream of mushroom soup
  • nan nan pepper, pepper
  • nan nan garlic powder, garlic powder

Instructions

Peel and thinly, slice potatoes. Brown hamburger, season. Grease a 2 quart baking dish, dust lightly with garlic powder. Alternate thin layers of potatoes, onion and hamburger, ending with hamburger; season each layer. Mix soup with milk. Pour over potatoes. Bake at 375* for 1 to 1 1/2 hour, or till done. Ten Minutes before removing from oven, sprinkle with cheese.
