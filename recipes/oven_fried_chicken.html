Oven Fried Chicken

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

5 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup flour, flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, butter
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper, pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika, paprika
  • 1 2-3 pounds broiler fryer chicken, broiler fryer chicken
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt

Instructions

Wash and dry chicken. Combine flour and seasonings in paper bay. Shake 2 or 3 pieces chicken at a time in flour mixture. In shallow pan in oven melt butter; remove pan from oven. Arrange chicken in pan in single layer, with skin side down. Bake, uncovered, 30 minute; turn chicken pieces with tongs; bake 15 minutes or until brown and fork tender.

CREAM GRAVY:Pour drippings from baking pan into measuring cup. Measure 1/2 cup fat into skillet; add 6 tablespoons flour (left over from coating chicken plus more flour as needed). Stir over low heat, loosening brown bits from baking pan, till smooth. Stir in 1 cup chicken broth and 1 1/2 cups half and half cream. Cook, stirring in a little water. Serve with chicken.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 113 Calories; 9g Fat (73.2% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 7g Carbohydrate; trace Dietary Fiber; 25mg Cholesterol; 520mg Sodium. Exchanges: 1/2 Grain(Starch); 0 Lean Meat; 2 Fat.
