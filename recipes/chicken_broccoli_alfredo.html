Chicken Broccoli Alfredo

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2/3 C. Magic Mix
  • 1 C. Water
  • nan nan Broccoli, Head Chopped
  • 2 nan chicken breasts
  • nan nan Parmesean

Instructions

In saucepan combine Magic Mix and water. Stir rapidly (I use a wire wisk) over medium heat until it starts to bubble.

Add 1/2- 1 cup of parmesan cheese per one cup of white sauce and a dash of salt and pepper. You can stop here and have alfredo sauce or add in 1/4-1/2 C. pesto to make a creamy pesto sauce.
