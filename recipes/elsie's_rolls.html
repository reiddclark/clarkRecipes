Ingredients

  • 4 cups milk, milk
  • 1 cup sugar, sugar
  • 5 teaspoons salt, salt
  • 5 nan egg, egg
  • 1 cup oil, oil
  • 4 packages yeast, yeast
  • 14 cups flour, flour

Instructions

Warm milk, add eggs, oil. Dissolve yeast in water. Add to milk, stir in 3 to 4 cups. flour, beat till smooth. Stir in rest. (Secret of good rolls is to keep rolls as soft as possible and still be able to knead them.)Let rest 10 min. Knead for at least 10 min. more if necessary. Let rise in warm place til double in bulk. Punch down, let rise again for 30 min. Roll out on floured board, shape as you please. Let rise until double. Bake at 375.This dough is very versatile, it makes wonderful cinnamon rolls, orange rolls or can also be refrigerated and used later.
