Instructions

Peel, rinse and cut potatoes lengthwise in 8th. Melt butter in square cake pan. Do not brown. Heat oven to 425Â°. Add potatoes, turn to coat with butter, bake for 30 minutes until partly browned on underside. Turn the potatoes and continue baking until they are quite soft on the inside and crisp on the outside. Sprinkle with salt and serve at once.
