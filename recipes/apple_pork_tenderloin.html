Apple Pork Tenderloin

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

5 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup half and half
  • 1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3/4 cup apple cider
  • 3 nan golden delicious apples
  • 1 nan red onion
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch

Instructions

1. Heat 1Tablespoon butter in skillet, add onion, cook til tender. Add apples; cook 1 to 2 minutes. Add 1 tablespoon cider, cook 30 seconds.
2. Heat remaining butter in skillet. Rub pork with 1/4 teaspoon thyme, add to skillet sear on all sides. Remove - add cider, cook over high heat until reduced by 1/2. Over medium low heat add broth, pork and apple; simmer. Turning pork occasionally until instant thermometer reads 160Â° - about 20 minutes. Remove pork to cutting board - cover with foil.
3. Mix half and half, mustard, cornstarch, salt, pepper and 1/2 teaspoon thyme - stir into apple mixture in skillet. simmer til thickened.4. Slice pork 1/4 in. thick. Arrange slices around edge of platter. Spoon apple sauce into center. Serve.
