Golden Chicken Nuggets

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 3 whole Chicken Breast, skinned boned
  • 1 nan egg, slightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup water, water
  • 1/2 cup flour, flour

Instructions

Cut chicken into 1 x 1 pieces. Mix egg, water, salt, and flour. Dip chicken into batter. Pour corn oil into pan. Carefully add chicken, fry 3-5 min. or until golden brown. Drain. Serve with barbecue sauce, LaChoy Sweet & Sour Sauce or nippy pineapple sauce.

NIPPY PINEAPPLE SAUCEMix 1/2c. pineapple preserves, 2 T. prepared mustard and 2 T. horseradish in saucepan. Cook 3 min. Makes 3/4 cup.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 298 Calories; 14g Fat (44.3% calories from fat); 32g Protein; 8g Carbohydrate; trace Dietary Fiber; 124mg Cholesterol; 368mg Sodium. Exchanges: 1/2 Grain(Starch); 4 1/2 Lean Meat; 0 Fat.
