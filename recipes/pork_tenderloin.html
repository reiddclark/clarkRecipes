Pork Tenderloin

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard, dry mustard
  • 2 pounds pork tenderloin, pork tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika, paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin, cumin
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce, soy sauce
  • 5 cloves garlic, sliced

Instructions

Mix all ingredient except pork. In a large zip lock bag place pork and marinade and let set in refrigerator for 30-60 minutes. Broil or grill 3 inches from heat for 10-12 minutes or until meat reaches temperature of 160*. Slice and enjoy.
