Chicken Salad

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup pecan, choppedoptional
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg, optional
  • 3/4 cup grapes, cut in half
  • 1/4 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt and nutmeg. Add everything else and let sit 2 hours.Serve this salad on lettuce leaf, croissant roll, or bagel. I use less mayonnaise, more pineapple..I also leave out the pecans and it still is excellent.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 172 Calories; 12g Fat (63.4% calories from fat); 11g Protein; 5g Carbohydrate; 1g Dietary Fiber; 33mg Cholesterol; 261mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Grain(Starch); 1 1/2 Lean Meat; 0 Vegetable; 0 Fruit; 1 1/2 Fat.
