Jalapeno Relish

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups carrots
  • 2 cups onion
  • 1 1/2 cups red pepper
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seed
  • 3 cups cider vinegar
  • 5 cups jalapeno
  • 2 1/2 cups sugar

Instructions

Chop onions and red pepper. Slice jalapenos. Grate carrots. Add rest of ingredients and boil for 15 minutes.Seeds of the jalapeno are what make the relish hot or not. If you want it hot add plenty of seeds, some if you want it medium and none if you want it mild.
