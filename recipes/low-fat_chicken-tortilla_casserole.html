Low-fat Chicken-tortilla Casserole

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese, grated
  • 2 101/2 oz can reduced fat cream of mushroom soup, reduced fat cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup nonfat milk, nonfat milk
  • 1 cup light sour cream, light sour cream
  • 1 7 oz can green chiles, diced
  • 1 dozen corn tortillas, cut in 1" strips
  • 6 nan boneless chicken breast, cooked and cut up
  • 1 1/2 cups monterey jack cheese, grated
  • 1 nan onion, chopped

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 9 by 13 inch pan with nonstick spray; set aside. Spray small pan with nonstick spray and cook onion over medium-high heat until softened, adding water or chicken broth if needed.In a large bowl, add onion to soup, milk, sour cream and green chiles.In prepared pan layer 1/3 of the tortillas, then 1/3 of the chicken, 1/3 of the sauce and 1/3 of the cheese. Repeat two or more times, ending with cheese.Bake 30 minutes, or until hot and bubbling around the edges.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 281 Calories; 15g Fat (47.1% calories from fat); 15g Protein; 23g Carbohydrate; 2g Dietary Fiber; 44mg Cholesterol; 331mg Sodium. Exchanges: 1 Grain(Starch); 1 1/2 Lean Meat; 1/2 Vegetable; 0 Non-Fat Milk; 2 Fat; 0 Other Carbohydrates.
