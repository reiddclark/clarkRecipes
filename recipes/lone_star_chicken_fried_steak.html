Lone Star Chicken Fried Steak

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

5 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds round steak, round steak
  • 1 cup flour, flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt
  • nan nan pepper, pepper
  • 2 nan egg, egg
  • 1/2 cup milk, milk
  • nan nan oil for frying, oil for frying

Instructions

Trim steak, tenderize, cut into 5 pieces. Combine flour, salt and pepper. Dredge all steak pieces in flour mixture until lightly coated. Combine beaten egg and milk. Dip steak into egg mixture and dredge again in flour. Heat 1/2 inch of oil in a heavy skillet. Place steaks in skillet and fry until golden brown on both sides. To make gravy, remove steaks to warm oven, retaining 6 tablespoons of drippings (or use bacon drippings). Add 6 tablespoons flour. Cook and stir until flour begins to brown. Add 3 cups hot milk and stir until thickened. Season with salt and pepper to taste and pour over warm steaks.
