Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips, milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 pound butter, butter
  • 1/4 cup water, water
  • 1/4 cup white Karo syrup, white Karo syrup
  • 1 cup chopped pecans, chopped pecans
  • 1 cup sugar, sugar

Instructions

Combine all ingredients except chocolate chips, in a heavy saucepan; cook at medium heat until mixture turns brown in color. Spread quickly on cookie sheet cool slightly, put chips on top and spread until candy is coated. Cook and break into pieces.
