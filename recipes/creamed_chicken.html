Creamed Chicken

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour, flour
  • 1 stick butter, butter
  • 1 can mushroom soup, mushroom soup
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, parsley
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth, chicken broth
  • 4 cups cooked chicken, cooked chicken
  • 1 1/2 cups milk, milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon accent® seasoning mix, accent® seasoning mix
  • 2 tablespoons onion, minced-dried

Instructions

Melt butter; add flour and stir until smooth. Combine remaining ingredients and add to flour mixture. Stir until thickened over low hear. Add chicken. Server over rice, noodles or frozen Pepperidge Farm patty shells.
