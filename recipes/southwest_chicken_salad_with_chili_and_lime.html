Ingredients

  • nan nan cumin, cumin
  • nan nan chili powder, chili powder
  • nan nan Mexican oregano
  • 4 nan avocado, slices
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped green chilies, finely chopped green chilies
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar, sugar
  • nan nan DRESSING, DRESSING
  • 1 nan finely minced green onion, finely minced green onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1/2 cup salsa, salsa
  • 4 nan chicken breast, chicken breast
  • 2 tablespoons seasoned rice vinegar, seasoned rice vinegar
  • 3 heads romaine lettuce, torn
  • 4 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 nan red onion, diced
  • 2 cups brown rice, cooked
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cilantro, finely chopped cilantro
  • 2 cups black beans, black beans
  • 4 nan fresh squeezed limes, fresh squeezed limes
  • 3 tablespoons canola oil, canola oil

Instructions

Season chicken with oregano, chili powder and cumin; cook on the grill. Toss romaine, tomatoes, and onion; arrange on four plates. Add salsa to rice and warm in the microwave. Next warm beans in microwave. Top each salad with half cup of rice and half cup of beans. Slice chicken into thin strips and place on top of salad. If desired, garnish with cilantro sprigs and avocado slices. Drizzle dressing over the top right before you're ready to serve.
