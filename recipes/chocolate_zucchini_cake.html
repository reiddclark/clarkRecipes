Instructions

Grate zucchini. Mix flour, baking powder, cinnamon, salt, soda and cloves.Cream oil and sugar, add eggs one at a time.Beat in melted chocolate.Add dry ingredients, beat till smooth.Fold in zucchini and chips and nuts.Bake in greased and floured bundt pan.Bake 350*, 1 hour 20 min. Cool 10 minutes before removing.
