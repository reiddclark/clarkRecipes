Yield

20 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar, powdered sugar
  • 2 cups sifted flour, sifted flour
  • 4 nan eggs, eggs
  • 2 cups sugar, sugar
  • 6 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons powdered sugar, powdered sugar
  • 2 sticks butter, butter
  • 4 tablespoons flour, flour

Instructions

Cream butter and 1/2 cup powdered sugar. Stir in 2 cups flour. Place in 9 X 13 inch pan. Bake 20 minutes at 350*. Beat eggs, 2 cups sugar and lemon juice for 5 minutes. Lightly mix or fold in 4 tablespoons flour. Bake 25 minutes at 350* . Sprinkle when done with powdered sugar.
