Country Style Chicken Kiev

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons basil leaves, basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon oregano leaves, oregano leaves
  • 2/3 cup butter, butter
  • 1/2 cup dried bread, crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley, chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions, chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup apple juice, apple juice
  • 6 nan chicken breast, chicken breast
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt, garlic salt

Instructions

Oven 3&5*. Melt butter. Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, basil, oregano, garlic salt. Dip chicken in butter, then coat with crumbs. Save butter. Place chicken skin side up in ungreased 10-inch pan. Bake 50-60 minutes or until chicken is fork tender. Meanwhile, add apple juice, green onion and parsley to reserved butter. When chicken is golden brown, pour butter sauce over chicken. Bake 3-5 minutes.
