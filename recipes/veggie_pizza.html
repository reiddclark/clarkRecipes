Veggie Pizza

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

12 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 packages Crescent Roll, Crescent Roll
  • 1 8 oz cream cheese, cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream, sour cream
  • 1 package ranch salad dressing, dry

Instructions

Unroll crescent rolls and cover large cookie sheet. Bake 15 min. or till light brown. Cool.

Mix cream cheese, sour cream and dressing mix. Spread over crescent crust. Cover with favorite veggies.Ideas... Shredded carrots, broccoli flowerets, shredded zucchini, red and green bell peppers, mushrooms. Or what ever sounds good to you.







Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 87 Calories; 9g Fat (88.0% calories from fat); 2g Protein; 1g Carbohydrate; 0g Dietary Fiber; 25mg Cholesterol; 61mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Lean Meat; 0 Non-Fat Milk; 1 1/2 Fat.
