Beet Bread

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 nan eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 15 ounce can sliced beets
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

Instructions

Puree beets with juice and transfer to large bowl. Stir in the sugar, oil, eggs and vanilla. In separable bowl, combine flour, cocoa, soda and salt. Add to beet batter and mix well. Stir in chips and nuts.Pour into 2 oiled 8x8x2 bread pans. They should be 3/4 full. Bake 350 for 30-40 min.
