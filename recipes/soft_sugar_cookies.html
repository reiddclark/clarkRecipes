Soft Sugar Cookies

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 6 cups flour, sifted
  • 1 cup sour cream, sour cream
  • 2 nan egg, egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla, vanilla
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda
  • 2 cups sugar, sugar

Instructions

Cream butter and sugar; add eggs, vanilla and sour cream; blend well. Add dry ingredients and beat well. Refrigerate for about an hour and then roll out 1/4-inch thick on floured board. Cut out shapes and bake at 350* for 10 - 12 minutes. When cookies are cooled, ice and decorate as you wish.

For old-fashioned sugar cookies sprinkle with sugar before baking.
