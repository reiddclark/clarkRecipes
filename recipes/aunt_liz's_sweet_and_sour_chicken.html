Aunt Liz's Sweet And Sour Chicken

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons catsup
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 nan egg, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 3 pounds chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock

Instructions

1. Sprinkle chicken with garlic salt, pepper. Let stand for one hour or more.
2. Preheat oven to 325*.
3. Dip chicken in beaten egg, then in flour. Brown in hot oil. Place in baking dish.
4. Cover with following sauce. Sauce: Mix sugar, vinegar, chicken stock, catsup and soy sauce in saucepan and heat. Pour over chicken; bake uncovered for 1 hour.
5. Turn chicken once or twice while baking.
