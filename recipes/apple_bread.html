Apple Bread

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups apples
  • 2 cups sifted flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sour milk
  • 2 nan eggs

Instructions

Cream sugar, butter. Mix soda with milk. Add vanilla, salt, sour milk, and eggs, to creamed mixture. Stir in apples and flour. Mix well. Sprinkle with topping. Makes 2 small or 1 large loaf. Bake in a greased and floured pan. Bake 1 hour at 325*.

Topping:2 Tablespoons butter2 Tablespoons flour2 Tablespoons sugar1 teaspoons cinnamon
