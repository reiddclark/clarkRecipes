Oatmeal Pancakes

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

16 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 2 cups buttermilk, buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon oil, oil
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour, whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 1 cup quick-cooking oats, quick-cooking oats
  • 2 nan egg, well beaten

Instructions

Combine flour, oats, brown sugar, soda and salt. Whisk in eggs, oil, and buttermilk and mix just until dry ingredients are well moistened; thin with water if needed. Spread batter on hot griddle, turning one.Yield: 16 4 inch pancakes.

*Buttermilk substitute: Put 2 tablespoons cider vinegar or lemon juice in 2 cup measure; add milk to equal 2 cups. Stir and let stand a few minutes to thicken.* To use dry milk: Mix 1/2 cup dry milk with dry ingredients. Put 2 tablespoons vinegar or lemon juice in cup; add water to equal scant 2 cups buttermilk.Note: Pancakes will be thinner when using either of these substitutions.

These pancakes are great with cider syrup and fried apples.
