Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. soda
  • 2 nan eggs, (2 T. Dehydrated egg powder + 1/4 C. Water)
  • 2/3 c. sugar
  • 1 c. flour, (half and half or all-purpose)
  • 1 c. yellow cornmeal
  • 1 c. buttermilk, (3 T. dry powdered milk + 1 C. Water and 1 T. lemon juice or vin
  • 1/2 c. butter/margarine

Instructions

Melt butter; add to the sugar and stir well. Add eggs and beat until well blended. Combine buttermilk and soda and stir into sugar-egg-butter mixture. Add cornmeal, flour, and salt and stir until just blended. Pour into greased 8-inch square pan. Bake at 375 degrees about 30 minutes or until it begins to pull away from sides of pan. Serve piping hot with butter and honey. Reid likes the middle not cooked 28-30 mins

Note: For glass pan, reduce heat to 350 degrees.
