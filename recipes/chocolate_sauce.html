Chocolate Sauce

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons corn syrup, corn syrup
  • 1/2 cup cream, cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar, sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter, butter
  • 1/2 square chocolate, chocolate

Instructions

Melt chocolate and butter. Add sugar, cream, and corn syrup. Bring to boil. Boil to soft ball stage. Add enough additional cream for right consistency.
