Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon white pepper, white pepper
  • 5 cloves garlic, garlic
  • 1/2 cup peanut oil, peanut oil
  • 1 cup soy sauce, soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dry mustard, dry mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dry ginger, dry ginger

Instructions

Mix marinade. Take chicken, remove fat, pour sauce over. Marinate covered with foil for 1 hour. Bake 1/2 hour, remove foil, baste, cook until done. Or can marinate over night. Shake every once in a while.
