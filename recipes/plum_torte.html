Plum Torte

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon, cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar, sugar
  • nan pinch salt, salt
  • 2 large eggs, eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder, baking powder
  • 1 cup unbleached flour, unbleached flour
  • 6 1/2 inch sli plums sliced, plums sliced
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter, unsalted butter

Instructions

Arrange a rack in the lower third of the oven. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.



In an electric mixer, cream the 3/4 cup of sugar and butter. Add the flour, baking powder, eggs and salt and beat to mix well. Place in a 9 or 10 inch ungreased springform pan. Cover the top with the plums. Mix the cinnamon with the remaining 1 or 2 tablespoons of sugar and spinkle over the top.

Bake for 40 to 50 minutes until the center tests done with a toothpick. Remove and cool to room temperature or serve warm with vanilla ice cream.

Note. The torte may be regrigerated or frozen for several months, well wrapped. To serve return to room temperature and reheat at 300 degrees until warm.

This works well with any stone fruit.
