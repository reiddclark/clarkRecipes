Doug's Chilli

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons cumin, cumin
  • 1 teaspoon whole oregano, whole oregano
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 10 pounds ground chuck, ground chuck
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper, crushed red pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground red pepper, ground red pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic, minced
  • 10 tablespoons chili powder, chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned pepper, seasoned pepper
  • 3 nan jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 1/2 cups stewed tomatoes, stewed tomatoes
  • 1 3/4 cups whole tomato, whole tomato
  • 2 cans tomatoe sauce, tomatoe sauce
  • 30 ounces water drained form boiled pinto beans
  • 1 teaspoon season-all, season-all
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt

Instructions

Brown ground chuck with next three ingredients. When brown, add the next 8 ingredients. Simmer 30 minutes before adding remaining ingredients. Simmer about 3 hours before serving.

Boil beans to make pinto bean water but don't use the beans for chili, use it for something else. True Texas chili doesn't have beans in it.
