Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese, grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream, sour cream
  • 3 ounces cream cheese, cream cheese
  • 6 nan green onion, green onion
  • 8 ounces pkg. broad noodles, pkg. broad noodles
  • 1 cup fresh sliced mushrooms, fresh sliced mushrooms
  • 2 8 ounce cans tomato sauce, tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar, sugar
  • nan dash pepper and salt, pepper and salt
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef, ground beef

Instructions

Brown hamburger, seasoning with salt, pepper, garlic powder and sugar. Add tomato sauce, and onions and simmer 15 minutes. Cook noodles; drain. Cream together cream cheese and sour cream. Layer meat mix, cheese mixture, noodles, three times and top with shredded cheese. Bake 350* for 20-30 minutes.
