Rice Krispies Treats

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

24 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup margarine, margarine
  • 4 cups miniature marshmallows, miniature marshmallows
  • 5 cups rice Krispies cereal, rice Krispies cereal

Instructions

Melt margarine in large saucepan over low hear. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Cook over low heat 3 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add Rice Krispies cereal. Stir until well coated. Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press mixture evenly into buttered 13 X 9 inch pan. Cut into squares when cool.
