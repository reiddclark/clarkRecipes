Peach Torte

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 2 tblspoons Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 6 small Peaches sliced
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 8 tblspoons Butter
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • nan pinch Salt

Instructions

Arrange a rack in the lower third of the oven. preheat the oven in 350.

In mixer cream 3/4 cup sugar and butter. add the flour, baking powder, eggs and salt and beat the mixture well. Place in 9 or 10 inch ungreased springform pan. cover the top with LOTS of peaches. Mix the cinnamon with the remaining 1-2 tabls sugar and sprinkle over the top.

Bake for 45-50 minutes until the center tests done. remoe and cool.

(This can be frozen for several months well wrapped, to serve return to room tempature and reheat at 300 until warm.)

This works well with any stone fruit, plum etc)
