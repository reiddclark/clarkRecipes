Great Lasagna

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cottage cheese, cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper, pepper
  • 10 ounces lasagna noodles, lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, flaked
  • 1 pound ground beef, ground beef
  • 2 nan egg, egg
  • 2 6 oz. can tomato paste, tomato paste
  • 1 lb can tomatoes, tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, salt
  • 1 tablespoon basil, basil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt

Instructions

Brown meat. Spoon off fat. Add next 5 ingredients. Simmer uncovered 30 minutes. Stir occasionally. Cook noodles, drain, rinse. Beat egg until foamy; add remaining ingredients except mozzarella. Layer 1/3 noodles in a 9x13 pan, 1/3 cottage cheese, 1/3 mozzarella cheese, 1/3 meat sauce. Repeat ending with sauce then sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Bake 375* 1 hour. If assemble before and refrigerated, bake 1 1/2 hours.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 515 Calories; 27g Fat (47.8% calories from fat); 35g Protein; 33g Carbohydrate; 2g Dietary Fiber; 125mg Cholesterol; 1386mg Sodium. Exchanges: 1 1/2 Grain(Starch); 4 Lean Meat; 1 1/2 Vegetable; 3 1/2 Fat.
