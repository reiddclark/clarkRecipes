Chicken, Broccoli And Rice Casserole

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 package broccoli
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 2 cups cooked chicken
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup corn flakes, crushed
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups rice

Instructions

In a buttered dish layer, 1) rice 2) chicken 3) broccoli. Mix soup, sour cream and grated cheese. Spread on top of rice, chicken and broccoli layers. Mix corn flakes with melted butter. Sprinkle over casserole and bake for 35-40 minutes at 350.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 3207 Calories; 131g Fat (37.4% calories from fat); 154g Protein; 341g Carbohydrate; 6g Dietary Fiber; 531mg Cholesterol; 2578mg Sodium. Exchanges: 21 1/2 Grain(Starch); 16 1/2 Lean Meat; 1/2 Non-Fat Milk; 20 1/2 Fat.
