Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef, lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin, ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano, dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup water, water
  • 2 teaspoons instant minced onions, instant minced onions
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder, chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch, cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed dried red pepper, crushed dried red pepper

Instructions

Combine all dry ingredients in a small bowl until evenly distributed. Brown ground beef in a medium skillet over medium-high heat. Drain excess grease. Add water and seasoning mix. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
