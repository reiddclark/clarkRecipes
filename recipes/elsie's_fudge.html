Ingredients

  • 2 squares baking chocolate, baking chocolate
  • 4 cups sugar, sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream, heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup water, water
  • 1/4 cup butter, butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 2 tablespoons light corn syrup, light corn syrup

Instructions

Put all ingredients into heavy sauce pan, stir thoroughly before putting over heat. This helps to keep the cream from curdling. Bring to boil and wash down the sides by putting on a lid for a minute or two. Stir to keep from burning. Cook to 232* pour out onto cold wet slab. Allow to cool until neither hot or cold. stir unto set up. As soon as candy sets up add nuts and flavoring. Leave out chocolate and add vanilla for vanilla fudge.

To make frosting. Use 4 cup sugar 1 cup 1/2 and 1/2, 1/2 cup cream 2 T. Karo, 2 T. butter, 2 sq. chocolate.Stir to boil, put on lid for 3 min. Wash down side with wet paper towel. Bring to 232* cool, stir. Micro low to melt and frost.
