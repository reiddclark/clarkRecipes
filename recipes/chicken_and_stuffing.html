Chicken And Stuffing

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups monterey jack cheese
  • 6 nan boned and skinned chicken breast halves
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 box stove top stuffing

Instructions

Wash chicken place in bottom of casserole dish. Salt and pepper. Cover with cheese, then cover with undiluted soup. Mix dressing as directed on package. Put on top of soup in dish. Bake 350* 1 hour.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 255 Calories; 11g Fat (41.0% calories from fat); 35g Protein; 2g Carbohydrate; trace Dietary Fiber; 95mg Cholesterol; 392mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Grain(Starch); 4 1/2 Lean Meat; 1 1/2 Fat.
