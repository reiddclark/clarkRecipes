Chicken Casserolet

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 14 1/2 oz Italian style stewed tomatoes
  • 2 15 oz. cans Great northern beans, rinsed drained
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 medium green or red pepper, cut into 1/2" pieces
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme, crushed
  • 8 ounces chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, snipped
  • 2 medium carrot, sliced into 1/2 inch
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
  • 1 nan bay leaf
  • 8 ounces turkey sausage 1/2 " pieces, halved lengthwise

Instructions

Rinse chicken and pat dry with paper towels. Cut into 1 inch pieces. Layer carrots, sweet pepper, onion. garlic, beans, undrained tomatoes, chicken, and sausage in a 3 1/2 -4 or 5 quart crock pot.Combine broth, parsley, thyme, ground red pepper, and bay leaf in a bowl. Add mixture into cooker.Cover; cook on low-hear setting for 7 to 8 hours. (Or, cook on high-heat setting for 3 1/2 to 4 hours.) Discard bay leaf.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 566 Calories; 5g Fat (7.7% calories from fat); 39g Protein; 93g Carbohydrate; 30g Dietary Fiber; 19mg Cholesterol; 303mg Sodium. Exchanges: 6 Grain(Starch); 3 Lean Meat; 1 Vegetable; 0 Fat.
