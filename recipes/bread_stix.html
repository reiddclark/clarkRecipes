Bread Stix

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon yeast
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 5 cups flour
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 cup cool water

Instructions

Melt butter in hot water. Add cool water and everything else except flour. Add 3 cups of flour and gradually add 1/2c more until bread is ready to handle. Usually between 4 to 6 cups. Knead 5 minutes. Let rise double then roll out 1/2- inch thick. Spread with butter and season salt. Cut into strips. Let rise for a minute, then bake at 375' until golden, about 10-15 minutes.
