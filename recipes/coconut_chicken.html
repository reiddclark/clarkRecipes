Coconut Chicken

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper, pepper
  • 1/4 cup coconut, coconut
  • 1 cup corn flake crumbs, corn flake crumbs
  • 6 nan chicken breast, boneless
  • 1 nan egg white, egg white
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, salt

Instructions

Wash chicken, pat dry. Cut into 2 inch strips.Mix coconut and corn flakes, add salt and pepper.Beat egg white till foamy. Dip chicken into egg white. Then into cornflake mixture. Fry in 1/4 hot oil. Or if you want it healthier then cook in oven for about 10-15 minutes. Or until done.Serve with pineapple sauce.

Make pineapple sauce by mixing 1/3 cup crushed pineapple, 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard and 1 teaspoons brown sugar. Combine 1 teaspoon cornstarch with 2 Tablespoons water, add to crushed pineapple, cook over medium heat until it thickens. Serve warm.
