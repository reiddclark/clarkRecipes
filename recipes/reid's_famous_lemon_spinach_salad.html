Reid's Famous Lemon Spinach Salad

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon Olive oil
  • 1 nan Lemon
  • 2 tablespoon Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Craisins
  • 1/4 cup Candied almonds
  • 1 nan Apple, sliced thinly

Instructions

1. Zest the lemon into a mixing bowl.
2. Slice the lemon in half and squeeze the juice into the bowl.
3. Add olive oil to bowl and whisk ingredients.
4. Add 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar to bowl. Add remaining 1/2 tablespoon to taste. Whisk.
5. Pour over spinach in a salad bowl. Add craisins, candied almonds, and apple slices.
6. Mix salad up and serve.
