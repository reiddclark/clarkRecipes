Roasted Vegetables

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 nan onion, onion
  • 5 nan whole garlic cloves, whole garlic cloves
  • 2 nan red potatoes, red potatoes
  • 2 nan sweet potato, sweet potato
  • 1 nan butternut squash, butternut squash
  • 5 nan carrot, carrot

Instructions

Peel sweet potatoes, squash, and carrots, and garlic. (Don't chop up garlic but keep them whole cloves, they become so sweet when they are roasted) Chop up all vegetables into equal size pieces. Place foil on a cookie sheet and spray it with cooking spray. Place all vegetables on the cookie sheet so that none of the vegetables overlap. Sprinkle salt on vegetables. I'll make two if I'm having company. Bake in 350* oven for 20 minutes. Turn vegetables over and bake for another 20 minutes. If desired, broil the vegetables for the last 3-5 minutes to make more roasted.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 135 Calories; trace Fat (2.1% calories from fat); 3g Protein; 34g Carbohydrate; 5g Dietary Fiber; 0mg Cholesterol; 24mg Sodium. Exchanges: 2 Grain(Starch); 1 Vegetable.
