Chewy Sesame Bread

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • nan nan sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dill weed
  • 1 tablespoon yeast

Instructions

Put sugar in water, add yeast. Add other ingredients, and mix well with a spoon. Cover with a towel and let rise 1 hour or until double in bulk. Spread out on 2 cookie sheets or dripper pans. Melt butter and pour over dough. Sprinkle sesame seeds. Bake at 375* for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
