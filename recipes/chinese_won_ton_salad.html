Chinese Won Ton Salad

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 nan green onion, diced
  • 1 can water chestnuts, sliced
  • 1 cup celery, diced
  • 1 head lettuce
  • 1/2 package won ton skins
  • 4 nan chicken breast
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds, toasted

Instructions

Cut won tons into 1/2 inch strips and fry in hot oil until lightly brown. Add rest of ingredients. Mix dressing and add right before serving.

DRESSING:1/4 cup oil4 Tablespoons sugar1 teaspoon salt1 Tablespoon soy sauce4 teaspoon rice vinegar
