Apple Pie

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 10 cups tart apples
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons flour

Instructions

If apples lack tartness sprinkle with about 1 tablespoon lemon juice. Combine sugar, flour, cinnamon, and dash salt; mix with apples. Line 9 inch pie pan with pastry. Fill with apple mixture; dot with butter. Adjust top crust, cutting slits for escape of steam; seal. Sprinkle with sugar. Bake at 400 for 50 minutes or until done.

I prefer granny smith apples.
