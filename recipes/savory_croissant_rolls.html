Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon milk, milk
  • 2 8 ounce cans Pillsbury croissant rolls, Pillsbury croissant rolls
  • 3/4 cup Italian bread crumbs, Italian bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons onion, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper, pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 2 cups chicken, cooked and diced
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, cream cheese
  • 3 tablespoons margarine, margarine

Instructions

Blend cream cheese and butter. Then add next six ingredients. Blend Well. Separate croissant rolls. Spoon 1/4 cup meat mixture on large end of triangle. Roll up and place on cookie sheet. Brush tops with melted butter. Sprinkle with bread crumbs. Bake 350* for 20 min or until brown. Be careful not to burn.
