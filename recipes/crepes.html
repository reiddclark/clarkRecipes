Ingredients

  • 2 nan egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

In bowl or blender, combine all ingredients and beat until smooth. Lightly
grease a 6-inch skillet. Heat. Remove from heat; spoon in about 2
tablespoons batter. Spread batter evenly. Return to heat; brown on one
side, turn over and brown other side
