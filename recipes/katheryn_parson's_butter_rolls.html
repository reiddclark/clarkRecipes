Katheryn Parson's Butter Rolls

Ingredients

  • 8 cups flour, flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt, salt
  • 2 tablespoons yeast, yeast
  • 2 cups milk, milk
  • 1 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1 cup butter, butter
  • 6 nan eggs, beaten

Instructions

Mix milk, sugar, and butter in bowl, heat until butter starts to melt. Make sure that milk is the temperature of a baby's bottle then add the yeast, 2 cups flour and salt. Mix throughly, add eggs and 2 more cups flour. Keep adding flour until all is added. Dough will be sticky.

Put in refrigerator over night. Take 1/2 of dough and divide that into thirds. Roll each third into a circle brush with melted butter. Cut into 1/8th roll up.Brush with butter again and let rise for 4 hours.Makes 48 rolls.

Bake 400Â°F for 10 minutes.

1/2 batch will fill a big cookie sheet.
