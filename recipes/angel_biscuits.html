Angel Biscuits

Description

No description

Tags

Side Dish, Vegetarian, American, Oven-Baked

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon salad
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 5 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 2 packets yeast
  • 2 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup shortening

Instructions

1. Dissolve yeast in 3 T. water.

2. Mix together and refrigerate overnight.

3. Pinch off and bake 10 minutes in a 400* oven.

4. Keeps two weeks in air tight container.
