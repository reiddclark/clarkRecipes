Ingredients

  • 6 nan egg whites, egg whites
  • 1/2 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1/4 cup water, water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar, cream of tartar
  • 1 1/4 cups flour, flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, baking powder

Instructions

Sift together flour, 1 cup sugar, baking powder and salt. In large mixing bowl, beat egg whites until frothy, add cream of tartar. Gradually beat in 1/2 cup sugar a little at a time, beat until whites form stiff not dry peaks. In small bowl combine egg yolks, water, vanilla, and sifted dry ingredients. Beat at medium high speed for 4 minutes or until mixture is light and fluffy. Fold yolk mixture gently but thoroughly into beaten egg whites. Turn into ungreased 10 inch tube pan. Bake at 350Â° for 45 minutes. Invert pan to cool.
