Poppy Seed Muffins

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup oil, oil
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds, poppy seeds
  • 3/4 cup milk, milk
  • 1 nan egg, egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder, baking powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour, flour
  • 1 teaspoon lemon peel, lemon peel

Instructions

Mix dry ingredients. Mix egg, milk and oil. Combine, stir in poppy seed and lemon peel. Grease 12 muffin cups. Bake 400*, 20 min or till golden.
