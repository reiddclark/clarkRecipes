Apple Pie Cake

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

15 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup nuts
  • 4 cups apple
  • 2 nan eggs
  • 1 cup oil
  • 2 cups sugar

Instructions

Mix oil, sugar, and eggs until smooth. Stir in flour, baking soda, cinnamon, vanilla and salt. Fold in apples. Bake in a 9 x 13 inch pan at 350* for 1 hour. Serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
