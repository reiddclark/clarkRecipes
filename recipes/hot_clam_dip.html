Hot Clam Dip

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream, sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 can minced clams, minced clams
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, mayonnaise
  • 1/4 pound sharp cheddar cheese, grated

Instructions

Mix all but clams and cheese with mixer until smooth. Add drained clams. Mix with spoon and pour into a Pyrex dish or glass pie plate. Sprinkle with cheese and paprika. Bake at 350* for 20 Minutes. Serve with carrots or tortilla chips.
