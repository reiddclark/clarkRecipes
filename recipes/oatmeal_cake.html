Oatmeal Cake

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour, flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon, cinnamon
  • 2 nan eggs, eggs
  • 1 teaspoon soda, soda
  • 1 cup light brown sugar, light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1/2 cup margarine, margarine
  • 1 cup quick-cooking oats, quick-cooking oats
  • 1 1/2 cups boiling water, boiling water
  • 1 cup sugar, sugar

Instructions

Pour boiling water over oatmeal and margarine. Mix well. Let stand 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Combine sugars with eggs; blend well. Add flour, soda, salt and cinnamon. Mix. Add oatmeal mixture. Pour into greased 9 x 12 inch pan. Bake at 350* for 30 to 40 minutes.

Oatmeal Cake Topping:

1/2 cup margarine1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts1 cup coconut1/2 cup sugar1/4 cup evaporated milk ( can use milk)1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Mix ingredients together and spread on warm cake. Place under broiler for 5 to 10 minutes or until brown.
