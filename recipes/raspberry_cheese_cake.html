Raspberry Cheese Cake

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 package yellow cake mix, yellow cake mix
  • 2 cups Cool Whip®, Cool Whip®
  • 2 cups powdered sugar, powdered sugar
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, cream cheese
  • 1 package raspberry danish dessert, raspberry danish dessert
  • 1 package frozen raspberries, frozen raspberries

Instructions

Make cake mix according to the directions on the package. Divide the batter evenly into two greased and floured 9 1/2 x 13" baking pans. While the cakes are baking, make one package of raspberry danish dessert using the liquid from the thawed raspberries for part of the water. Let the pudding cool, then fold in the drained raspberries. Blend together the cream cheese and powdered sugar. Fold in the cool whip and blend very well. Spread this over the cooled cakes. Then spread the cooled danish dessert over the top and chill. Cut and serve. (This can be made a day ahead of when it will be used.)
