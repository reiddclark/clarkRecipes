Italian Zucchini

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon sugar, sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice, lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons margarine, margarine
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry basil, crushed
  • 3 small zucchini, sliced
  • 1 large tomato, peeled cut-wedges
  • 1/3 can onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup water, water

Instructions

Melt margarine in large saucepan. Saute' onion until tender. Mix in tomato, basil, zucchini, sugar, water, lemon juice. Cover; simmer 10 min.
