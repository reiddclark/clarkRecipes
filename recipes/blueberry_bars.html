Blueberry Bars

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 can 21 oz blueberry pie filling
  • 4 nan eggs
  • 1 cup margarine
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

Instructions

Cream sugar and butter; add eggs and vanilla; stir in flour, baking powder, and salt. Spread 1/2 dough in 10x15 pan; gently spread pie filling over dough; sprinkle nutmeg over pie filling; dot remaining dough over pie filling, spreading to cover as much as possible; bake at 350* for 45 min,; glaze while warm with 1 1/4 c. powdered sugar, 1 T. melted butter, and 2 T. lemon juice.

Cherry Bars: Substitute cherry pie filling for blueberries and almond extract for lemon juice.
