Peach Cobbler

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons baking powder, baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon, cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 3/4 cup milk, milk
  • 1 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1/2 cup margarine, margarine
  • 1 can peaches, peaches
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 1 cup flour, flour

Instructions

Melt margarine in baking dish while mixing batter. When margarine is melted, pour batter over it and then spoon on the peaches. Bake at 350* until brown or 1 hour. I use 1 qt. undrained peaches with 1 cup frozen blueberries or raspberries.
