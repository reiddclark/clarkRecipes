Jam Bars

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups quick-cooking oats, quick-cooking oats
  • 1 3/4 cups sifted flour, sifted flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda
  • 1 cup brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 1 cup shortening, half butter
  • 1 1/2 cups jam, jam

Instructions

Mix oats, flour, soda and sugar. Cut in shortening until mixture is crumbly. Divide into 2 parts, pack 1/2 firmly in bottom of oiled 7 1/2 x 11" baking pan. Spread jam evenly on top, then add remaining dough and pack firmly. Bake in moderate oven 350*, 40 min. Cool thoroughly.
