Red Potato Salad with Green Beans and Basil

Description

No description

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 t. sea salt
  • 3/4 t. dijon mustard
  • 1/2 t. freshly ground pepper or grains of paradise
  • 3 T. white or red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 bunch of green onions, sliced (use the greens and the whites)
  • 2 nan handfuls of basil, (about a half a bunch), cut in a chifonade
  • 1 lb. green beans, cut into 1 1/2 inch lengths
  • 3 nan lbs. red potatoes, scrubbed and cut into 1 inch dice

Instructions

This salad can be served immediately or chilled for later.
