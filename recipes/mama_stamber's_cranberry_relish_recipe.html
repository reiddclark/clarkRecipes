Mama Stamber's Cranberry Relish Recipe

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar, sugar
  • 2 cups raw cranberries, raw cranberries
  • 3/4 cup sour cream, sour cream
  • 1 small onion, onion
  • 2 tablespoons horseradish, horseradish

Instructions

Mix the ingredients and freeze. On Thanksgiving morning, move the mixture to refrigerator to thaw. Makes 1 1/2 pints.
