French Bread

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

15 Servings

Ingredients

  • 6 cups flour, flour
  • 2 cups hot water, hot water
  • 1/2 cup water, water
  • 2 tablespoons yeast, yeast
  • 3 tablespoons sugar, sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt, salt
  • 1/2 cup oil, oil

Instructions

Dissolve 1/2 warm water with 2T. yeast. Put in large bowl;water, sugar, salt and oil. Add 3 cups flour, beat well. Add: yeast mixture, beat well. Add 3 cups flour. Beat well, use a spoon. Leave in bowl. Let rest 10 min. Beat down with spoon. Repeat 5 times. On the 5th time divide dough into two parts. Roll out into a 12-14" rectangle. Roll up like jelly roll. Put on oiled cookie sheet with seam side down. Let rise 30 min. Bake 375* for 30 min. Makes 2 loaves.
