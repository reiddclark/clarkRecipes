Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 6 nan egg, egg
  • 1 cup milk, milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 cup flour, flour
  • 4 Tablespoons margarine, margarine

Instructions

Combine flour, salt, eggs and milk. Heat oven to 425*. Place the butter or margarine in a 9 x 13 inch baking pan in oven. When butter is melted, pour the flour mixture over it. Do not stir. Bake for 25 minutes. Or until brown. Cut and serve with syrup.
