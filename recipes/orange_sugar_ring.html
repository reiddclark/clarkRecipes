Orange Sugar Ring

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 teaspoon orange peel, grated
  • 2 packages refrigerator biscuits, refrigerator biscuits
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg, nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice, orange juice

Instructions

Separate biscuits; combine butter and orange juice; mix remaining ingredients in a separate bowl; dip biscuits in butter, then in sugar mixture; stand upright in well greased bundt pan; bake at 450* for 12-15 min.; turn out onto plate. Don't over bake.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 109 Calories; 5g Fat (37.3% calories from fat); trace Protein; 17g Carbohydrate; trace Dietary Fiber; 12mg Cholesterol; 50mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Grain(Starch); 0 Fruit; 1 Fat; 1 Other Carbohydrates.

NOTES : To make it less sweet but still good just half the sugar and dip every other roll in sugar.
