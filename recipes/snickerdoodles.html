Snickerdoodles

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 2 Tablespoons cinnamon, cinnamon
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar, sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda
  • 2 2/3 cups unsifted flour, unsifted flour
  • 2 nan eggs, eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar, sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar, cream of tartar

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400*. In mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt. Shape dough into balls. Combine sugar and cinnamon; roll ball of dough in sugar mixture. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets. Flatten slightly. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until eggs are set.
