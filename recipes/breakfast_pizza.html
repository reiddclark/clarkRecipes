Breakfast Pizza

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1 cup frozen loose pack hash brown potatoes, thawed
  • 1 8 ounce pkg refrigerated crescent rolls
  • 5 nan eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound pork sausage

Instructions

Cook sausage in medium skillet until brown; drain and set aside. Separate crescent dough into 8 triangles. Place with elongated points toward center of greased 12 inch pizza pan. Press bottom and sides to form a crust. Seal perforations. Spoon sausage over dough. Sprinkle with hash brown potatoes and cheese. Combine eggs, milk, salt and pepper; pour over sausage mixture. Bake at 375* for 25 minutes.
