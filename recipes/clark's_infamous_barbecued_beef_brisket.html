Instructions

Soak mesquite in water to cover for 1 hour; drain. Prepare barbecue grill, lighting fire at one end only; rub meat with dry rub. When coals are white, place meat over coals; sear for 5 min. on each side; move meat to side of grill away from fire; spread 4 c. mesquite over coals; cover grill. Smoke brisket for 1 hour, keep temperature about 200*; sprinkle mesquite with water occasionally; spread rest of mesquite over coals; continue smoking meat for 1 more hour. Preheat oven to 200*; wrap meat tightly with heavy duty foil; bake for 8 hours; slice meat across grain; serve with barbecue sauce.
