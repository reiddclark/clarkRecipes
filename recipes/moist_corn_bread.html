Moist Corn Bread

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buttermilk, or milk + 1 T. Vinegar
  • 1/3 cup sugar, sugar
  • 2 nan egg, egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 cup flour, flour
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine, butter or margarine
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda
  • 1 cup cornmeal, cornmeal

Instructions

Melt butter; add sugar; mix well; add eggs, beating well after each; mix soda with buttermilk; add to egg mixture. Mix dry ingredients; add to liquid mixture; mix briefly. Bake in greased 8 x 8 pan at 350* for 30 - 35 min.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 270 Calories; 13g Fat (43.3% calories from fat); 5g Protein; 34g Carbohydrate; 2g Dietary Fiber; 78mg Cholesterol; 344mg Sodium. Exchanges: 1 1/2 Grain(Starch); 0 Lean Meat; 2 1/2 Fat; 1/2 Other Carbohydrates.
