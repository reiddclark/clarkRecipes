German Apple Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 2 nan eggs, eggs
  • 2 teaspoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk, milk
  • nan nan powdered sugar, powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar, sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon, cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons butter, butter
  • 1 small granny smith apple, granny smith apple
  • 1/2 cup flour, flour

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400*. Spray small oval baker with nonstick cooking spray. For apple filling, peel, core and slice apples. Melt butter over medium heat. Add apple, brown sugar and cinnamon. Cook 20 minutes or until apple is tender and most of liquid has evaporated.Meanwhile, for pancake, combine flour, sugar and salt in bowl. Whisk together eggs, milk and melted butter. Add egg mixture to flour mixture; whisk until dry ingredients are moistened. Pour batter over apples in oval baker. Bake 20 minutes or until puffed and golden brown.

I cook apples in a iron skillet and then just mix the batter all together in a blender and pour over apples and bake in skillet. When done flip onto a plate and sprinkle with powered sugar and serve with whipped cream.
