Japanese Chicken

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water, water
  • 2 tablespoons butter, butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, brown sugar
  • nan nan flour, flour
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar, cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, ginger
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce, soy sauce
  • 4 nan boned and skinned chicken breasts, boned and skinned chicken breasts
  • 10 ounces crushed pineapple, crushed pineapple

Instructions

Cut chicken into bite size pieces. Sprinkle chicken with ginger, then dredge with flour. Brown chicken in butter. Mix water, soy sauce, vinegar, and brown sugar. Pour sauce and then pineapple over chicken. Turn down heat to low. Cover and cook for 30 to 45 min. Serve over rice.
