Aunt Amy's Granola

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw mixed nuts, (no roasting or salting)
  • 2 t ground nutmeg
  • 3 T ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 cup Amaranth, (this is a whole grain)
  • 1/4 cup Chia Seed
  • 1/4 cup raw pumpkin seeds, (no roasting or salting)
  • 1/2 cup organic hemp seed, (doesn't have to be ground like flax seed for the body to use)
  • 1 cup raw wheat germ
  • 8 cups organic rolled oats, (not quick, that means they've been processed more)
  • 1 cup organic raw shredded coconut, (this has NO sweeteners)
  • 1/4 nan raw sesame seeds

Instructions

1. Put all these ingredients in a small pot on the stove and warm them until the oil melts and they're all combined. It takes less than 5 minutes.

2. While your liquids are combining in a VERY large bowl combine:

3. Stir all your dry ingredients together in the bowl until well combined. If you have little kids like me, it's a good idea to "pulse" your mixed nuts in a food processor to make the pieces smaller and easier for them to chew.

4. Take the wet and pour small amounts over your granola mix and stir until well combined and moistened. Spread the granola equally on 2 jelly roll size pans (they are bigger than a 9x13 cookie sheet) and bake at 250 degrees for 45 minutes, stirring the granola around half way through the cooking time.

5. When your granola is cooked and cooled, I add dried UNSWEETENED fruits like raisins, cranberries, apples, bananas, dates, figs, cherries, papaya, pineapple, etc. Serve with milk, preferably Almond Milk!!
