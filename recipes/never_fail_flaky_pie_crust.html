Never Fail Flaky Pie Crust

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons water, water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vinegar, vinegar
  • 1 nan egg, egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 2/3 cup shortening, shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups flour, flour

Instructions

Mix flour and salt cut in shortening until fine crumb like texture. Beat egg, water and vinegar; add flour mixture and roll into 2 pie crusts.
