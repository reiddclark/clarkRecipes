Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 package chocolate chip
  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 4 nan eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups shortening

Instructions

Beat sugars and shortening until creamy. Add everything but chips, mix well. Bake 350*, till done. Best if not over cooked.
