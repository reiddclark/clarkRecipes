Clam Chowder

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup margarine
  • 1 can minced clams
  • 1 pint half and half
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 4 cups cubed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion

Instructions

Drain juice from clams and pour over vegetables; add water to cover; simmer until tender (about 20 minutes). Melt margarine in sauce pan; stir in flour; add half and half; add milk; cook until thickened; add to vegetables along with seasonings and clams. Heat but do not boil.
