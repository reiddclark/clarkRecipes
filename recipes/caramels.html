Caramels

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Dark Karo syrup
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together and cook until temperature reaches 242*. Butter a 9x9 glass dish. Pour mixture into dish. Do not pour the last little bit or caramel into pan. Pour it onto a separate plate ( it is the last of the pan and will be harder). Cool and cut into squares. Wrap in wax paper.

TAR BARS:Add 7 to 10 drops of anise to caramel toward the end of cooking. Black food coloring could also be added.

I like 10 drops.
