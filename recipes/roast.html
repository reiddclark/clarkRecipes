Instructions

place roast in crock pot, salt and pepper the roast

peel carrots and potatoes

cut up carrots, potatoes, and onions

place cut up vegetables in crock pot

mix the cream of mushroom soup and the onion soup mix and half a cup of water

pour mixture over the roast and vegetable mix

put on lid

cook on high for 4-6 hours
