Waffle Cookies

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups flour, flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder, baking powder
  • 3/4 cup shortening, shortening
  • 3/4 cup sugar, sugar
  • 3 ounces strawberry jello, strawberry jello
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 3 nan eggs, eggs

Instructions

Cream shortening and sugar, add vanilla. Add eggs, salt, flour, baking powder, and jello. Mix to a soft dough. form into small balls and place about 2" apart in waffle iron (between low and medium heat). Bake a few minutes until done. Don't cook too long or they will be tough.
