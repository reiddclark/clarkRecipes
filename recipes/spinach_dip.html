Spinach Dip

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

15 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise, mayonnaise
  • 1 package Hidden Valley Salad Dressing mix, Hidden Valley Salad Dressing mix
  • 1 package frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup sour cream, sour cream

Instructions

Mix sour cream, mayonnaise and dressing mix. Add spinach and mix well. Let chill for a hour then place in hollowed out round rye bread.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 140 Calories; 16g Fat (94.6% calories from fat); 1g Protein; 1g Carbohydrate; trace Dietary Fiber; 12mg Cholesterol; 99mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Vegetable; 0 Non-Fat Milk; 1 1/2 Fat.
