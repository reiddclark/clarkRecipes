Royal Frosting

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 3/4 cups powdered sugar, powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 3 nan egg whites-room temperature, egg whites-room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar, cream of tartar

Instructions

Combine egg whites, powdered sugar, vanilla, and cream of tartar. Beat with electric mixer 7 to 10 minutes or till frosting is very stiff. Keep frosting covered with damp cloth at all times to prevent crust from forming. Makes 3 cups
