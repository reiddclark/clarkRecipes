Taco Dip

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

12 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper, pepper
  • 1 cup grated cheese, grated cheese
  • 1/2 cup green onion, green onion
  • 1 small can sliced olives, sliced olives
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 cup sour cream, sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise, mayonnaise
  • 1 package taco seasoning mix, taco seasoning mix
  • 2 cups refried beans, refried beans
  • 2 medium tomato, tomato
  • 3 medium avocados, avocados
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice, lemon juice

Instructions

Peel, pit and mash avocados, mix with lemon juice, salt and pepper. Next combine sour cream, mayo and 1/2 taco seasoning. Mix refried beans with rest of taco seasoning. Spread bean dip on platter, top with avocado mixture and then sour cream mixture. Sprinkle with onions, tomatoes, olives, and tip with cheese. Serve with tortilla chips.
