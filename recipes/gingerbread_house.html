Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup molasses, molasses
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, ginger
  • 3 1/2 cups flour, flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter, butter
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon, cinnamon

Instructions

Cream sugar, butter. Add rest and roll 1/4 in. thick. Cut into shapes and bake. Make 15 min at 350*. Take out after 10 minutes to place crushed lifesavers for windows. Easier to roll out in large cookie sheet, cut and then remove excess. Bake.

Use royal frosting for glue to hold it all together.
