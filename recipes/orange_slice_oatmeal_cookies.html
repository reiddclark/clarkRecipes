Orange Slice Oatmeal Cookies

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 cup coconut
  • 1 1/2 cups oatmeal
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup candy orange slices
  • 1 nan egg

Instructions

Reserve 1/2 cup flour. Put cut up orange slices into reserved flour. Sift remaining flour, baking powder and soda and salt together. Cream shortening and sugar, beat in egg and vanilla. Stir in sifted dry ingredients. Add oatmeal, coconut, and floured orange pieces. Mix well. Drop by teaspoon onto cookie sheet or roll into ball and press out. Bake 350Â° for 8 - 10 minutes.
