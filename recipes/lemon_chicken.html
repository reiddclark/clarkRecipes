Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 nan boned and skinned chicken breast, boned and skinned chicken breast
  • nan nan salt and pepper to taste, salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cups chicken broth, chicken broth
  • 2 nan lemon's juiced, lemon's juiced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter, butter
  • nan nan flour for dredging, flour for dredging

Instructions

1. Pound chicken breast with meat pounder to uniform thickness. Dredge lightly in flour, shaking off excess.2. In a large saute pan over medium-high hear, melt the butter and oil until it sizzles. Add the chicken breasts and saute, turning once or twice until cooked through and juices run clear. Remove chicken and set aside. 3. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minutes until the mixture boils.4. Add lemon juice to the chicken stock and whisk into saute pan. Reduce heat to a simmer.5. Return chicken to pan to heat through, thickening sauce to desired consistency. Season to taste with salt and ground white pepper.6. Serve the chicken on a bed of basmati rice, and spoon the sauce over the chicken. Garnish with chopped parsley and lemon slices.
