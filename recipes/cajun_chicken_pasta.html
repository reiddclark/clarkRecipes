Cajun Chicken Pasta

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces linguine, cooked and drained
  • 4 large mushroom
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • nan nan Parmesan cheese
  • 2 nan Zucchini
  • 2 nan boned and skinned chicken breasts, sliced thin
  • 1 nan green onion
  • 2 nan red and green bell peppers
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

Place chicken and Cajun seasoning in a Ziploc to coat. In large skillet over medium heat, saute chicken in butter until almost tender. Add peppers, mushrooms and onions; cook and stir 2-3 minutes. Reduce heat, add cream and seasoning; heat through. Add linguine and toss-heat through. Sprinkle with cheese.

Liz doubles all but cream.

Good with yellow squash added.
