Pulled Pork Sandwiches

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • nan nan barbecue sauce, barbecue sauce
  • nan nan sourdough buns, sourdough buns
  • 5 pounds whole pork butt, whole pork butt
  • nan nan salt and pepper, salt and pepper

Instructions

Place pork butt in crockpot or large kettle on stove. Cook until very tender and breaks apart easily with fork. Season with salt and pepper. Remove from crockpot when done and remove all fat. Return shredded pork back to crockpot and cover with your favorite barbecue sauce and simmer for one hour. Serve on sourdough buns or regular hamburger buns.
