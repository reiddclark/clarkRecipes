Everyday Waffles

No description

Tags

Yield

30 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 nan stiffly beaten egg whites, stiffly beaten egg whites
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder, baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 2 nan beaten egg yolks, beaten egg yolks
  • 1 3/4 cups milk, milk
  • 1/2 cup oil, oil
  • 1 3/4 cups sifted flour, sifted flour

Instructions

Sift together dry ingredients. Combine yolks, milk and oil; stir into dry ingredients. Fold in whites, leaving a few fluffs. Bake. Makes 3 10-inch waffles.
