Instructions

Preheat oven to 375*. Grease and flour 10 inch tube pan. Streusel - Combine 2 tablespoons of dry cake mix with bworn sugar and cinnamon in medium bow. stir in Peacans, set aside.Cake - combine remaining cake mix, eggs, sour cream, oil, water and sugar in large bowl. Beat at medium speed with electric mixer for 2 minutes. Pour 2/3 of batter into pan. sprinkle with streasel filling. Spoon remaining batter evenly over filling. Bake at 375* for 45-55 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cook in pan 10 minutes. Invert onto cooling rack. Cool completely. Glaze - Combine powdered sugar and milk in small bowl. Stir until smooth. Drizzle over cake.
