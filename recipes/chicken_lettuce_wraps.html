Chicken Lettuce Wraps

No description

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 8 nan dried shiitake mushroom, optional
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 head lettuce leaves, seperated
  • nan nan cucumber, sliced thin
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • nan nan chineese cellophane noodles, cook as directed
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 8 ounces water chestnuts, drained and minced
  • 8 ounces bamboo shoots, drained and minced
  • 2 small dried chiles, optional
  • 2 nan green onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon apple juice
  • nan nan shredded carrots
  • 1 teaspoon fresh minced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 teaspoons cornstarch - divided
  • 2 teaspoons apple juice
  • 1 1/2 pounds boned and skinned chicken breast, sliced
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

Prepare the sauce. Sauce - 1/2 cup water, 1/4 cup sugar, 2 Tbsp. soy sauce, 2 Tbsp rice vinegar, 2 Tbsp ketchup, 1 Tbsp, lemon juice, 1 Tbsp chinese hot mustard, 1 - 3 tsp. garlic chili paste, 1/8 tsp. sesame oil. Dissolve the sugar in the water in a small bowl. Add the remaining ingredients and mix well. Refrigerate until ready to serve the lettuce wraps.

Cover mushrooms with boiling water and let stand for 30 minutes.

Combine hoisin sauce, soy sauce, 1 Tbsp apple juice, oyster sauce, 2 Tbsp. water, sesame oil, sugar and 2 tsp. cornstarch; set aside.

In a medium bowl, combine remaining 1 tsp. cornstarch, 2 tsp apple juice, 2 tsp. water, salt and pepper; lay chicken into mixture and spoon over to coat. Stir in 1 tsp vegetable oil and let sit 15 minutes to marinate.

Heat a wok or large skillet over medium high hear; stir - fry chicken in 3 Tbsp. vegetable oil for 3 - 4 minutes; remove from wok/pan chop chicken and set aside.

Heat 2 Tbsp vegetable oil in wok/pan; add ginger, garlic, onions, and chiles; stir - fry for 1 minute; add mushrooms, bamboo shoots, and water chestnuts; stir fry for 2 minutes.

Return chicken to wok/pan; pour in reserved mixture, simmer until thickened and heated through.

Spoon mixture into lettuce leaves; add sauce and roll up like a burrito.

Eat and and enjoy. This is good with cellophane noodles, grated carrots, and sliced cucumbers ( that have marinated in apple cider vinegar for a few minutes).
