Bow Ties, Zucchini, Carrots And Chicken

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 nan zucchini
  • 8 ounces chicken broth
  • nan nan lemon pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cube chicken bouillon cube
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 ounces bow tie pasta
  • 1/2 pound carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

Heat oil in skillet over medium heat, and cook 2 cloves garlic 1 minute, until golden brown. Place zucchini in the skillet, season with salt and pepper, cook until tender. Set aside. Place chicken in the skillet and cook 10 minutes, or until juices run clear.Place the carrots in a pot with enough water to cover, and bring to a boil. Cook until tender, drain, and set aside.Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.Melt butter in skillet over medium heat, cook the remaining garlic until golden brown. Pour in the chciken broth and water, stir in bouillon until dissolved. Gradually stir in parmesan cheese. Mix through. Gently toss with pasta to serve.
