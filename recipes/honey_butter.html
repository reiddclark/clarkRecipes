Honey Butter

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

30 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound butter, butter
  • 1 pound honey, honey
  • 1 pint Marshmallow cream, Marshmallow cream

Instructions

In electric mixer whip butter about 2 min. Add honey and whip together until well blended, next add marshmallow creme and blend well.Have all ingredients at room temperature. I store it in the refrigerator when not being served.This make a lot. Can easily be halved.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 178 Calories; 12g Fat (59.5% calories from fat); trace Protein; 19g Carbohydrate; trace Dietary Fiber; 33mg Cholesterol; 129mg Sodium. Exchanges: 2 1/2 Fat; 1 Other Carbohydrates.
