Sweet And Sour Dressing

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

100 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups salad oil, salad oil
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar, sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt, salt
  • 3 tablespoons onion juice, onion juice
  • 3 tablespoons poppy seeds, poppy seeds
  • 2/3 cup white vinegar, white vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons dry mustard, dry mustard

Instructions

Mix sugar, mustard, salt and vinegar. Beat fast while adding onion juice, and oil. (add oil slowly until mixture thickens and is creamy) Add poppy seeds last.
