Yield

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • nan nan salt and pepper, salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons tomato sauce, tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms, sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons butter, butter
  • 1 cup whipping cream, whipping cream
  • 4 ounces noodles, fettucine or linguini
  • 1/2 cup broccoli flowerets, broccoli flowerets

Instructions

Cook noodles in boiling water in medium saucepan until 1/2 done (about 4 minutes). Drain and return to pan. Add cream, butter, garlic, tomato sauce and broccoli and bring to boil over medium heat about 4-5 minutes. When noodles are done, stir in mushrooms to heat. Remove and sprinkle with cheese. Salt and pepper. Toss and serve.
