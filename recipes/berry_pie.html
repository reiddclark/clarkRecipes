Berry Pie

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar plus 2 tablespoons
  • nan nan butter
  • 2 lbs frozen blackberries or raspberries
  • nan dash salt
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch

Instructions

Mix together sugar, cornstarch and salt. Add mixture to frozen blackberries or raspberries. Let sit for 15 minutes. Pour mixture into unbaked crust, dot with butter, and cover with top crust. Cut air vents. Seal and flute edges. Bake at 450 F. for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 F. and bake for 30-40 minutes or until juice looks thickened. Be sure bottom of crust is done.

Blueberry PieUse berry pie recipe except decrease sugar to 3/4 cup and use blueberries.
