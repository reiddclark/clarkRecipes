Barbecue Beef

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons catsup
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 nan onion, chopped
  • 1 can tomato soup

Instructions

Brown meat and onion, then add remaining ingredients. Simmer about 15-20 minutes. Serve hot on hamburger buns as a sandwich.
