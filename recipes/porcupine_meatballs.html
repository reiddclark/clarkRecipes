Porcupine Meatballs

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water, water
  • 1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce, worcestershire sauce
  • 4 nan carrots, sliced
  • nan dash pepper, pepper
  • 2 tablespoons onion, minced
  • 1 tablespoon snipped parsley, snipped parsley
  • 1 nan egg, slightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup rice, rice
  • 1 pound ground beef, ground beef
  • 1 can condensed tomato soup, condensed tomato soup
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt

Instructions

Combine meat, rice, egg, parsley, onion, salt, pepper, and 1/4 cup condensed tomato soup. Mix thoroughly; shape in about 20 small balls and place in skillet. Mix remaining soup, water, and Worcestershire sauce; pour over meatballs, add sliced carrots. Bring to boil; reduce heat; cover and simmer 30 - 40 minutes, stirring every once in a while.
