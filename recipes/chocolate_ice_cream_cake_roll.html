Chocolate Ice Cream Cake Roll

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

20 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 package devil's food cake mix, devil's food cake mix
  • 4 nan egg, egg
  • nan nan ice cream topping, ice cream topping
  • 1/2 cup water, water
  • 2 quarts ice cream, softened

Instructions

Mix cake mix, water, and eggs. Beat 2 minutes at high speed. .Heat oven to 350*. Grease 2 (15 x 10- inch) jelly roll pans; line with waxed paper and grease again. Spread half the batter in each prepared pan. Bake one cake at a time on middle rack of oven. Bake 14 minutes. Immediately invert cake onto clean kitchen towels; that have been generously sprinkled with powdered sugar. Remove waxed paper. Starting at shorter end of cake, roll up cake in towel. Cool completely. Carefully unroll cake, but do not lay flat; remove towel. Spread each cake with 1 quart ice cream and roll up loosely. Wrap in foil; freeze until firm.To serve, cut into 1-inch slices. Drizzle with fudge topping. Store in freezer.

I bake in 12x17 inch pans, for 10 minutes.
