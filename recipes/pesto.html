Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic, garlic
  • 3 tablespoons pine nuts or walnuts, toasted and chopped
  • 3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup olive oil, olive oil
  • 1 cup basil leaves, basil leaves

Instructions

To make the pesto:In a blender or small food processor blend together all ingredients with salt and pepper to taste until smooth. Pesto keeps, covered and chilled, up to 1 week. Makes about 2/3 cup.
