Crown Jewel Jello

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 3 ounce pkg jello - line, cherry orange lemon
  • 1/4 cup sugar, sugar
  • 2 cups cool whip, cool whip
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice, pineapple juice

Instructions

Prepare the first three flavors of Jell-O using 1 cup boiling water and 1/2 cup cold water. Pour each flavor into a 8" square pan and chill until very firm. Then combine the lemon Jell-O, sugar and 1 cup boiling water. Stir until dissolved completely. Put in pineapple juice. Chill until slightly thickened. Then cut all the Jell-O into small squares. Blend cool whip with lemon Jell-O mixture. Put cubes in and chill 3-4 hours.
