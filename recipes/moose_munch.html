Ingredients

  • 1 pound white, milk or dark chocolate
  • 1 cup butter, butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 1 pound nuts, nuts
  • 6 quarts popped corn, popped corn
  • 2 cups brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup light corn syrup, light corn syrup

Instructions

Place popped corn in extra large bowl. In a large saucepan melt butter over medium heat. When melted stir in brown sugar, corn syrup and salt. Stirring constantly until reaching a rolling boil. Stop stirring and let boil for 5 minutes. Immediately remove from heat and stir in vanilla and baking soda. Mixture will foam up. Pour immediately over popped corn. Place on large jelly roll pan and allow to coll. Melt chocolate in a double broiler or in the microwave. Add nuts at this time them so that they are well coated or else they won't stick to the popped corn. Pour melted chocolate over popped corn and begin to mix until well coated. Let cook and place in an airtight container. This recipe will stay fresh for one week.If the pop corn is still sticky place it in a warm oven until dry.
