Instructions

Instructions
Dissolve 1/2 warm water with 1 T. yeast. Put in large bowl;water, sugar, salt and butter. Add 3 cups flour, beat well. Add: yeast mixture, beat well.. Beat well, use a spoon. Leave in bowl. Let rest 10 min. Beat down with spoon. Repeat 5 times. Let rise 30 min. Bake 375* for 30 min.
