Caramel Corn

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

20 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions

Mix first three ingredients in pan bring to boil, add can of sweetened condensed mild. Boil to soft ball stage, add vanilla, pour over 6-8 qt. popped corn.
