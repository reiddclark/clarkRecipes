California Marinade

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • nan nan pepper
  • nan nan salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

Place all ingredients in screw-top jar; shake thoroughly. Pour over meat; marinate 1 hour. Grill, baste with marinade occasionally.
