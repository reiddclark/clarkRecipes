Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups sugar, sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons butter, butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 1 can 20 oz. pineapple rings in juice, drained
  • 2 cups flour, flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 4 nan egg whites or 2 eggs, egg whites or 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk, buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350degrees. Spray 9x 13 inch pan with nonstick spray.Place 2 tablespoons butter in pan and place in oven to melt; remove pan as soon as it is melted. Sprinkle brown sugar over bottom of pan. Evenly distribute pineapple rings over brown - sugar mixture. Set aside.Combine flour and baking soda in a small bowl and st aside.In large bowl, combine 1/4 cup butter and sugar until mixed well. Add egg whites one at a time. beating 30 seconds after each addition. Add vanilla extract and mix well.Add half of flour and soda mixture, then half of buttermilk, mix and repeat.Spoon batter over pineapple - do not stir - then smooth the top. Bake 40 minutes, or until sharp knife inserted in center comes out clean. Allow cake to cool 10 minutes, then turn upside down onto serving platter. Remove any pineapple or sugar that sticks in pan and place it back on cake.

To make it a bit more fattening and a little bit more gooey. Increase butter to 1/4 cup and brown sugar to 1/2 cup for the gooey bottom.
