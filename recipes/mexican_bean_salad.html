Mexican Bean Salad

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

0 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 nan clove crushed garlic
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 nan red onion, chopped
  • 1 nan dash hot pepper sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 nan (10 ounce) package frozen corn kernels
  • 1 nan red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 nan green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 nan (15 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 nan (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 nan (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine beans, bell peppers, frozen corn, and red onion.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, lime juice, lemon juice, sugar, salt, garlic, cilantro, cumin, and black pepper. Season to taste with hot sauce and chili powder.
3. Pour olive oil dressing over vegetables; mix well. Chill thoroughly, and serve cold.
