Instructions

Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Pour milk into a small bowl. Pour bread crumbs into a shallow bowl. Dip ravioli into milk for 10 to 15 seconds. Lightly coat ravioli with bread crumbs. If necessary, add more milk and or bread crumbs to bowls. Place coated ravioli on a sheet of waxed paper or parchment paper. Spray ravioli on both sides with cooking spray. Place on cookie sheet. Bake 14 ro 15 minutes, turning halfway through baking time and spraying with cooking spray if needed. Serve hot sprinkled with parmesan cheese, with pasta sauce for dipping.
