Ingredients

  • nan nan coarse salt, coarse salt
  • 1 nan egg, egg
  • 4 cups flour, flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water, warm water
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar, sugar
  • 1 envelope dry yeast, dry yeast

Instructions

In a large bowl. mix yeast and sugar in warm water; let sit 5 minutes

Stir in salt and 2-3 C. flour for soft batter



Keeping your hands dusted with flour, knead inenough additional flour to make a dough stiff enough to roll out.

Still keeping your hands dusted, take a lump of dough about the size of a peach, and roll it between your hands to make an 8"or 12' rope about as thick as your finger.

Twist the rope into a pretzel and place it on a greased cookie sheet, brush it with a beaten egg, and sprinkle coarse salt on top. Repeat until all dough is used up.



Bake at 425* for 12-15 min. or until golden brown.
