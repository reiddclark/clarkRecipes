Pico De Gallo

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 large anaheim pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 bundle green onions and stalks, chopped
  • 1 can rotel, rotel
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt

Instructions

Mix all ingredients and place in covered bowl in refrigerator for 5 - 6 hours. Serve on fajitas, or tacos.
