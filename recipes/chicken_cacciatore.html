Chicken Cacciatore

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 3 pounds chicken, cut up
  • 1 can tomatoe sauce, 8 oz
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 cups thinly sliced onion
  • 1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can tomatoes, 16 oz
  • 1 can mushroom, 3 oz. sliced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon oregano

Instructions

Wash chicken and pat dry. Melt shortening in large skillet. Coat chicken pieces with flour. Cook chicken in shortening over medium heat 15 to 20 minutes or until light brown. Remove chicken; set aside. Add onion rings, green pepper and garlic to skillet; cook and stir over medium heat until onion and pepper are tender. Stir in remaining ingredients. Add chicken to sauce. Cover tightly; simmer 30 to 40 minutes or until thickest pieces are fork-tender.



Wendy Paterson uses: 2 cans spaghetti sauce plus 1 package of seasoning.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 769 Calories; 52g Fat (60.5% calories from fat); 9g Protein; 68g Carbohydrate; 6g Dietary Fiber; 0mg Cholesterol; 2140mg Sodium. Exchanges: 3 Grain(Starch); 3 1/2 Vegetable; 10 1/2 Fat.
