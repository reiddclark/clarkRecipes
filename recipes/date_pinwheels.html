Ingredients

  • 4 cups sifted flour, sifted flour
  • 1 cup chopped nuts, chopped nuts
  • 1 cup water, water
  • 1 cup sugar, sugar
  • 2 1/4 cups chopped dates, chopped dates
  • 1/2 cup raisins, raisins
  • 1 cup shortening, shortening
  • 2 cups brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 3 nan eggs, eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon, cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt

Instructions

Combine dates, raisins, sugar, water in pan; cook over low heat till thick and add the nuts and cool. Cream shortening, sugar, add eggs. Sift together dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture, mix well. Chill thoroughly. Divide the mixture into 2 parts, roll out to rectangles little less than 1/2" thick. Spread each with some date filling and roll as for jelly roll into two long rolls. Chill again over night, if possible. Cut with sharp knife or thread. Bake at 375Â° till light brown around edges.
