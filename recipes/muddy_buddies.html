Muddy Buddies

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips, semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter, peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup margarine, margarine
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar, powdered sugar
  • 9 cups Chex or Crispex, Chex or Crispex

Instructions

1. Pour cereal into large bowl; set aside.2. Melt, chips, peanut butter, margarine in microwave on high 1 to 1 1/2 minutes or until smooth, stirring after 1 minute. Stir in vanilla.3. Pour chocolate mixture over cereals, stirring until all pieces are evenly coated. Pour cereal mixture into a jumbo size Ziploc bag with powdered sugar. Seal securely and shake until all pieces are all coated.
