Soba And Slaw Salad With Peanut Dressing

Yield

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound chicken, coarsely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons Thai chile paste with garlic, Thai chile paste with garlic
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter, creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil, canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons dry roasted peanuts, chopped
  • 3/4 cup green onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups grated carrot, grated carrot
  • 6 cups shredded red cabbage, shredded red cabbage
  • 6 ounces uncooked soba noodles, broken in half
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce, soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar, rice vinegar

Instructions

Cook noodles according to package directions, omitting salt and fat. Drain, rinse with cold water.Combine noodles, shredded cabbage, carrot, 1/2 cup green onions, and chicken in a large bowl. Combine soy sauce and next 4 ingredients (through chili paste) in a small bowl. stir with a whisk until blended. Add soy sauce mixture to noodle mixture, tossing gently to coat. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup green onion and peanute.Good served with fresh lime to squeeze on salad.
