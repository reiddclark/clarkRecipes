Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup onion, onion
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream, sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise, mayonnaise
  • 1 package frozen petite peas-10, frozen petite peas-10
  • 1/4 cup bacon, bacon
  • 1 cup diced celery, diced celery
  • nan nan lettuce, lettuce
  • 1 can sliced chestnut, sliced chestnut

Instructions

Layer in clear bowl, water chestnuts, a layer of celery, and peas (do not cook). Stir mayonnaise and sour cream and frost top of salad like a cake. Be sure to seal edges. Sprinkle with sugar and sprinkle with cheese and bacon. Let set overnight or at least 6 hours.
