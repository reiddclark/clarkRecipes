Chicken Casserole

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 small pkg. Pepperidge farm stuffing
  • 1 nan chicken, stewed and boned
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 can cream of celery soup
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup

Instructions

Place cooked chicken in 9 x 13 inch pan. Mix soup and sour cream together and spread on top of chicken. Cover with the stuffing and top with melted butter and chicken broth. Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour at 350*. Freezes well.







Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 596 Calories; 48g Fat (74.0% calories from fat); 35g Protein; 4g Carbohydrate; trace Dietary Fiber; 217mg Cholesterol; 600mg Sodium. Exchanges: 0 Grain(Starch); 4 1/2 Lean Meat; 0 Vegetable; 0 Non-Fat Milk; 7 Fat.
