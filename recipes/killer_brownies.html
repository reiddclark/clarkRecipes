Killer Brownies

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup melted butter, melted butter
  • 1/2 cup cocoa, cocoa
  • 2 cups sugar, sugar
  • 4 nan eggs, eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour, flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts, chopped nuts
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla

Instructions

Melt butter, add sugar and cocoa. Beat in eggs one at a time. Add flour and vanilla.Pour into 9x13 greased pan; sprinkle on nuts; bake at 350* for 25-30 minutes.
