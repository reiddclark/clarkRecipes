Almond Braid

Tags

Vegetarian, Bake, Desserts, Bread

Yield

30 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons yeast
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups warm milk
  • 1 cup margarine
  • 6 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup rolled oats
  • 2 teaspoons almond extract

Instructions

  1. Place margarine and 3/4 c. sugar in 4 qt. bowl; pour heated milk over margarine; mix yeast with 1/2 c lukewarm water and 1 T. sugar; stir; let stand for 5 min.
  2. Add eggs and yeast to other mixture; add salt and sifted flour; beat together with spoon—no kneading; cover with cloth; let rise for 1 1/2 hours in warm place.
  3. Separate dough into 6 equal pieces. Mix filling of butter, sugar, oats, and almond extract.
  4. Roll out dough into an 8 X 10 rectangle; spread small amount of filling in center of dough.
  5. On each side of rectangle make cuts 2" apart and 2" long, lace strips by alternately crossing them over; tuck ends underneath; let rise for 30 min.; bake at 375°F for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Makes 6 pastries.
