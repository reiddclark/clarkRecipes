Lemon Pie

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups hot water, hot water
  • 1 tablespoon butter, butter
  • 3 nan egg yolks, egg yolks
  • 2 nan grated lemon rind and juice, grated lemon rind and juice
  • 7 tablespoons cornstarch, cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup cold water, cold water
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar, sugar

Instructions

Mix 1/2 c. cold water and cornstarch. Boil hot water and sugar. Add cornstarch. Boil hot water and sugar. Add cornstarch and cook until thick and clear, stirring constantly. Stir small amount into egg yolks. Add to mixture and cook 3 minutes longer. Remove from stove and add lemon rind, juice and butter. Cool stirring occasionally. Pour into pie shell. Top with meringue.

Meringue: 3 egg whites, 6 T. sugar, 1 tablespoon lemon juice. Beat egg whites until peaked add sugar gradually, fold in lemon juice. Brown.
