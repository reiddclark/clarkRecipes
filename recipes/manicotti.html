Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package manicotti noodles, manicotti noodles
  • 1 large jar spaghetti sauce, jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 tablespoon parsley flakes, parsley flakes
  • 2 tablespoons italian seasoning, italian seasoning
  • nan nan pepper, pepper
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage, Italian sausage
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef, ground beef
  • 4 slices bread, bread
  • 1 cup milk, milk
  • 2 nan eggs - beaten, eggs - beaten
  • nan nan salt, salt

Instructions

Par boil noodles 10 min. Cool in noodle tray. Beat eggs and milk with fork, soak bread in this mixture - set aside. Brown beef and sausage. Add salt and pepper. Italian seasoning and parsley. Mix 1/2 cheese into egg, milk and bread mixture. Pour into meat mixture, stuff noodles with meat filling. Place stuffed noodles in greased casserole, side by side. Pour sauce over the top. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake at 350* for 30 minutes.
