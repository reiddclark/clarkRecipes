Instructions

Beat eggs, sugar, margarine, and water until fluffy; sift flour, cocoa, soda, and cinnamon; add to mixture; mix well. Fold in nuts, chips, and vanilla; spoon into greased and floured tube pan; bake at 3258 for 60-70 min.
