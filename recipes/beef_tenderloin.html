Beef Tenderloin

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds beef tenderloin
  • 1/4 cup Spade L Ranch Beef Marinade and seasoning

Instructions

Rub meat with spices, wrap in foil and refrigerate overnight.Preheat oven to 500*. Bake tenderloin at 400 for 30 minutes.Mix juices with a little bit of horseradish and a little sour cream to make a good sauce to serve with meat.
