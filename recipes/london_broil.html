Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic, garlic
  • 1 cup soy sauce, soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water, water
  • 3 pounds london broil, london broil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar, sugar

Instructions

Fork london broil to tenderize. Mix all other ingredients in a gallon Ziploc bag. Marinate steak for up to 3 hours. Broil or BBQ and cut into thin strips to serve.
