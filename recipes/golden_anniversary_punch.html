Golden Anniversary Punch

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

20 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 6 ounce can frozen lemonade concentrate, frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 1 6 ounce can frozen pineapple juice, frozen pineapple juice
  • 1 12 ounce can apricot nectar, chilled
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice, lemon juice
  • 1 quart orange sherbet, orange sherbet
  • 2 large bottle ginger ale, chilled
  • 1 6 ounce can frozen orange juice concentrate, frozen orange juice concentrate

Instructions

Add water to frozen concentrates according to directions on cans. (If frozen pineapple juice is unavailable, use 1 large can of pineapple juice.) Add Chilled apricot nectar and lemon juice. Just before serving, spoon in sherbet. To keep carbonation, carefully pour ginger ale down the side of the bowl.
