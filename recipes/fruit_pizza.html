Yield

20 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla, vanilla
  • 1 large scoop cool whip, scoop cool whip
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1 batch sugar cookie dough, sugar cookie dough

Instructions

Spread a 1/4 inch thick layer of cookie dough in large cookie sheet. Bake until light brown. Cool.Mix sugar, cream cheese, vanilla, and cool whip. Spread over cookie and cover with favorite fruit.

Ideas. Kiwi, strawberries, blueberries, pineapple, peaches.
