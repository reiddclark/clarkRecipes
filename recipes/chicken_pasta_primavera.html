Chicken Pasta Primavera

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless chicken breast halves
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup Heavy whipping cream
  • 16 ounces Broccoli Florets, cauliflower and carrots thawed
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 7 ounces Pasta, spiral pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions

Cook pasta according to package directions. Saute chicken and garlic in butter. Add vegetables and cream. Cook until vegetables are tender. Add cooked pasta, parmesan cheese, salt and pepper. Cook until heated through. Serve
