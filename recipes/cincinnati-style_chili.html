Cincinnati-style Chili

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

5 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 can 8 oz. tomato sauce, tomato sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef, ground beef
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder, chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 can 15 oz kidney beans, kidney beans
  • 1 pkg 7 oz uncooked spaghetti, uncooked spaghetti
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can 16 oz. tomatoes, undrained

Instructions

Cook and stir ground beef and about 3/4 cup of the onions in 3 quart saucepan until beef is brown and onions are tender; drain. Stir in chili powder, salt, tomatoes, beans and tomato sauce; break up tomatoes. Cook uncovered over medium heat until desired consistency, about 10 minutes.Cook spaghetti as directed on package; drain. For each serving, spoon about 3/4 cup beef mixture over 1 cup hot spaghetti. Sprinkle each serving with cheese and onion.





Per Serving (excluding unknown items): 514 Calories; 32g Fat (56.0% calories from fat); 30g Protein; 27g Carbohydrate; 10g Dietary Fiber; 101mg Cholesterol; 949mg Sodium. Exchanges: 1 1/2 Grain(Starch); 3 1/2 Lean Meat; 1/2 Vegetable; 4 1/2 Fat.
