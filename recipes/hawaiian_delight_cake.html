Hawaiian Delight Cake

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

24 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whipped cream, whipped sweetened
  • nan nan coconut, coconut
  • 1 8 oz. cool whip, cool whip
  • 2 1 lb. cans crushed pineapple, crushed pineapple
  • 1 8 oz. packag cream cheese, cream cheese
  • 2 1/2 cans milk, milk
  • 1 3 oz. packag vanilla instant pudding, vanilla instant pudding
  • 1 package yellow cake mix, yellow cake mix

Instructions

Beat cream cheese with 1/2 cup of the milk. Add rest of milk and then pudding mix; beat and spread over cooled cake. Chill 30 minutes. Drain juice completely from pineapple. Put on top of pudding. Add whipped cream to cool whip and blend together. Spread over tip of cake and sprinkle with coconut.

Make cake according to package directions and bake at 350* in 12 x 16 inch pan for 15-20 minutes.
