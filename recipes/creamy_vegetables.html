Creamy Vegetables

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk, milk
  • 1 1/2 ounces cream cheese, cream cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt., salt.
  • 1 bunch broccoli, broccoli
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese, grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter, butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour, flour
  • 6 nan carrot, carrot
  • 1 nan onion, onion

Instructions

Peel carrots and slice into thin carrot sticks. Cut broccoli into small bite size pieces. Slice onions very thin. Cook in pressure cooker until pressure just begins to build. Or steam until just barely done. Drain vegetables and place in a 9x13 dish.. .Sauce. Melt butter add flour and stir well. Add milk and cook until thickened. Add salt and softened cream cheese. Pour over vegetables then sprinkle with grated cheese and then bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
