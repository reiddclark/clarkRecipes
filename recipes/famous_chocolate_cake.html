Yield

16 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cup flour, flour
  • 1 cup boiling water, boiling water
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla, vanilla
  • 2 teaspoons soda, soda
  • 1/2 cup sour milk, sour milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa, cocoa
  • 1/2 cup shortening, shortening
  • 2 cups sugar, sugar
  • 2 nan eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, salt

Instructions

To make sour milk, put 2 tablespoons lemon juice in milk. Let stand 5 minutes.

Put all ingredients, except boiling water, in large bowl. Mix until barely moistened. Add boiling water and mix until smooth. Pour into 9 X 13 inch pan. Bake at 350* for 30 to 35 minutes.
