Monkey Bread

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

10 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 2 cans refrigerator biscuits, refrigerator biscuits
  • 1/2 cup sugar, sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon, cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup margarine, margarine
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar, granulated sugar

Instructions

Remove biscuits from can; cut each into four pieces; combine sugar and cinnamon in plastic bag; place pieces of dough into bag; shake; remove the pieces from bag; stand on edge in a greased bundt pan. Bring to a boil the margarine, granulated sugar, and brown sugar; pour over the biscuits; bake at 350* for 20 min. or until done; cool slightly; turn onto a serving plate.
