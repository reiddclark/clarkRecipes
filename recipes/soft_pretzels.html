Instructions

In a large bowl, mix yeast and sugar in warm water; let sit 5 minutes.Stir in salt and 2 - 3 cups flour for soft batterKeeping your hands dusted with flour, knead in enough additional flour to make a dough stiff enough to roll out.Still keeping your hands dusted, take a lump of dough about the size of a peach and roll it between your hands to make an 8" to 12" rope about as thick as your finger.Twist the rope into a pretzel and place it on a greased cookie sheet, brush it with a beaten egg, and sprinkle coarse salt on top. Repeat until all dough is used up.Bake at 425Â°F. for 12 - 15 minutes or until golden brown. Cover loosely.
