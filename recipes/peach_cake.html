Peach Cake

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

12 Servings

Ingredients

  • 4 nan peaches, peeled and diced
  • 2 cups flour, flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda, baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk, buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup margarine, margarine
  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar, brown sugar
  • 1 nan egg, egg

Instructions

Cream margarine and brown sugar; stir in egg; add sifted dry ingredients alternately with buttermilk; stir in peaches. Bake in 9 x 13 greased pan at 350* for 30 -35 min. while still hot, add topping of 1/4 cup sugar and 1 teaspoon cinnamon.
