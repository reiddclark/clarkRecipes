Stir Fry Beef And Broccoli With Oyster Sauce

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons oyster sauce, oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce, soy sauce
  • 1 Pound flank steak, flank steak
  • 2 tablespoons chicken broth, chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar, light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil, toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch, cornstarch
  • 6 medium garlic cloves, minced or pressed
  • 1 small red bell pepper, cored seeded and diced
  • 1/3 cup water, water
  • 1 tablespoon apple juice, apple juice
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger, minced fresh ginger
  • 3 tablespoons peanut or vegetable oil, peanut or vegetable oil
  • 1 1/4 pounds broccoli, florets cut into bite size piece

Instructions

Combine the beef and soy sauce in a medium bowl; cover with plastic wrap and regrigerate at least 10 minutes or up to 1 hour, stirring once. Meanwhile, whisk the apple juice, chicken broth, oyster sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, and cornstarch in a measuring cup. Combine the garlic, ginger, and 1 1/2 teaspoons of the peanut oil in a small bowl.

Drain the beef and discard the liquid. Heat 1 1/2 teaspoons of the peanut oil in a nonstick 12 inch skillet over high heat until smoking. Add half of the beef to the skillet and break up the clumps; cook with stirring, 1 minute, then stir and cook until the beef is browned about the edges, about 30 seconds. Transfer the beef to a medium bowl. Add 1 1/2 teaspoons of the peanut oil to the skillet, heat until just smoking, and repeat with the remaining beef.

Add 1 Tablespoon of the oil to the now - empty skillet; heat until just smoking. Add the broccoli and cook 30 seconds; add the water, cover the pan, and lower the heat to medium. Steam the broccoli until tender-crisp, about 2 minutes; transfer to a peper towel-lined plate.

Add the remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons oil to the skillet; increase the heat to high and heat until just smoking. Add the bell pepper and cook, stirring frequently, until spotty brown, about 1 1/2 minutes.

Clear the center of the skillet; add the garlic and ginger mixture to the clearing and cook, mashing the mixture with a spoon, until fragrant, 15 to 20 seconds, then stir the mixture into the peppers. Return the beef and broccoli to the skillet and toss to combine. Whisk the sauce to recombine, then add to the skillet; cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce is thickened and evenly distributed, about 30 seconds. Transfer to a serving platter. Serve with rice.
