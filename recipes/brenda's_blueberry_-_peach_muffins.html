Brenda's Blueberry - Peach Muffins

Description

No description

Tags

Yield

1 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup peaches, peeled and diced
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 nan eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup blueberries

Instructions

Mix flour, sugar, B powder and salt. Add butter, beaten eggs, and milk, stirring just until blended. Carefully fold in fruit. Fill greased muffin tins and bake 400* for 18-20 min. Brush top with melted butter and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
