Minestrone Hamburger Soup

Yield

8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1/8 tablespoon pepper, pepper
  • 1 pound can tomatoes, tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 quarts water, water
  • 2 teaspoons salt, salt
  • nan nan basil, basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme, thyme
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 2 cups potatoes, diced
  • 1 pound ground beef, ground beef
  • 1 cup onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup rice, rice
  • nan nan beef bullion, beef bullion

Instructions

Brown beef and onion in large pan. Add potatoes, carrots, celery, cabbage and tomatoes. Bring to boil. Sprinkle rice into mixture. Add remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer 1 hour.
